Overscroll Behavior

.ease-overscroll-{auto, contain, none}

Controls what happens when scrolling past the boundary of a scrollable area. Critical for modals, sidebars, and nested scroll containers to prevent scroll chaining.

ClassDescription
ease-overscroll-autoDefault — scroll chaining allowed
ease-overscroll-containPrevent scroll chaining — inner scroll boundaries stay contained
ease-overscroll-noneNo overscroll effect or chaining

Example use cases:

  • ease-overscroll-contain on modals to prevent background page scroll
  • ease-overscroll-none on sidebars to prevent browser pull-to-refresh
  • ease-overscroll-x-none to disable horizontal overscroll navigation
