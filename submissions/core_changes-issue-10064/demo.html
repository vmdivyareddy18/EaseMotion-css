All images below use ease-aspect-square with a landscape source (distorted without object-fit).

Real-World Example

Card with ease-aspect-video + ease-object-cover:

Video Title

Thumbnail uses ease-object-cover to fill the 16:9 aspect ratio without distortion.

HTML Structure

<!-- Cover image in aspect-square container -->
<img class="ease-aspect-square ease-object-cover ease-w-full"
     src="wide-photo.jpg" alt="Cover" />

<!-- Contain + top alignment -->
<img class="ease-h-48 ease-object-contain ease-object-top"
     src="tall-artwork.png" alt="Artwork" />

<!-- Video thumbnail in 16:9 card -->
<div style="aspect-ratio: 16/9;">
  <img class="ease-object-cover" src="thumb.jpg" />
</div>
