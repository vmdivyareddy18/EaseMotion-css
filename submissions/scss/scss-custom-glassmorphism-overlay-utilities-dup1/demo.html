Glassmorphism Utilities

Beautiful, frosted glass overlays utilizing CSS backdrop-filter to add depth to your UI.

.ease-glass-light

Light Frosted Glass

Standard white glass overlay. Perfect for dark mode backgrounds or highly vibrant images. Balances readability with background visibility.

.ease-glass-heavy

Heavy Blurred Glass

A stronger blur with higher opacity. Ideal for modals or popovers where you want to heavily obscure the background content to focus attention.

.ease-glass-dark

Dark Frosted Glass

A sleek, black-tinted glass overlay. Commonly used in premium UI designs, MacOS-style dashboards, or over very light backgrounds.

.ease-glass-subtle

Subtle Glass

Minimal opacity and a very low blur radius. Great for sticky navigation bars or subtle card highlights where you barely want a frosted effect.
