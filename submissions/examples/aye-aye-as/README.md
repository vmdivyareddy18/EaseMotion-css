# Aye Aye

A pure CSS and HTML aye-aye clinging to a trunk at night, tapping the bark with its long skeletal middle finger to find a grub.

## What it shows

- The famous middle finger tapping on a very fast keyframe while the arm reaches on a slow one, so percussive foraging reads at two speeds at once
- Knock rings pulsing out of the bark in time with the taps
- Shaggy fur from a fine repeating conic gradient over the body and bushy tail
- Huge reflective eyes from layered radial gradients, ears swivelling independently, and a grub drawn out of the hole
- No images, no JavaScript, no external dependencies

## Usage

Open `demo.html` in any modern browser.

## Accessibility

All motion stops under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.
