# 🐦 Angry Birds

A classic Angry Birds clone built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Launch birds from a slingshot, destroy structures, and eliminate all pigs using physics-based projectile motion!

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Click and drag** the bird on the slingshot
2. **Aim** by pulling back (further = more power)
3. **Release** to launch the bird
4. **Destroy structures** and hit pigs
5. **Clear all pigs** to advance to the next level!

## ⌨️ Controls

| Action | Input |
|--------|-------|
| Aim/Launch | Click and drag on bird |
| Launch | Release mouse/touch |
| Next Level | Click "Next Level" or press `N`/`Enter` |
| Reset Level | Click "Reset" or press `R` |

## ✨ Features

- 🎯 **Physics-Based Projectile Motion** – Realistic trajectory with gravity
- 🐷 **3 Levels** – Increasing complexity with more pigs and structures
- 🏗️ **Destructible Structures** – Blocks break on impact
- 💥 **Chain Reactions** – Falling blocks can hit pigs
- 🎨 **Beautiful Visuals** – Colorful birds, pigs, and environments
- 📱 **Responsive Design** – Works on desktop and mobile
- 🖱️ **Drag-to-Launch** – Intuitive slingshot controls
- 📊 **Score Tracking** – Points for each pig eliminated
- 🏆 **Level Progression** – Advance through increasing difficulty

## 🎯 Scoring

- **+50 points** for each pig destroyed
- **+100 points** for completing a level

## 📊 Level Progression

| Level | Birds | Pigs | Complexity |
|-------|-------|------|------------|
| 1 | 3 | 1 | Simple structure |
| 2 | 4 | 2 | Two towers with bridge |
| 3 | 5 | 3 | Complex multi-level structure |
