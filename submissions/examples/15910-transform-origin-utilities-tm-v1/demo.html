transform-origin-utilities

An interactive visual guide to transform-origin utilities. Change the anchor point of elements to alter the pivot point of rotations, scaling, and 3D swing paths.

Gauge Pivot Point

Change the rotation origin on the gauge needle. Pinned at the bottom, it sweeps like a speedometer; pinned at the center, it spins like a propeller.

Hover Zoom Anchor

3D Hinge Axis Swing

Change the origin of a 3D rotation. Placing the origin on the left hinge swings the door inward; placing it on the right hinge swings it the opposite way; placing it on the top hinge swings it upward like a flap.
