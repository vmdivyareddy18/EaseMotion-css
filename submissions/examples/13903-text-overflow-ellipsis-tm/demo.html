Three-Line Clamp

Three-line clamping gives you a bit more breathing room while still maintaining the compact card feel. This technique uses -webkit-line-clamp: 3 combined with -webkit-box-orient: vertical to achieve browser-native truncation that is performant and accessible. Perfect for blog post excerpts, user reviews, and comment previews.
