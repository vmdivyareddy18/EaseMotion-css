🐢 .ease-bounce-slow 1.5s duration

Preview Classes Behaviour
⬇️ .ease-bounce .ease-bounce-loop .ease-bounce-fast Fast infinite loop (0.4s)
🔴 .ease-bounce .ease-bounce-slow .ease-bounce-high .ease-bounce-loop Slow high infinite loop (1.5s, 30px)
📦 .ease-bounce .ease-bounce-hover .ease-bounce-high High bounce on hover only
⬇️ .ease-bounce .ease-bounce-loop .ease-delay-500 Starts after 500ms delay, then loops
display: inline-block is set automatically by .ease-bounce so transform works on inline elements like <span> and <a> without extra CSS.

prefers-reduced-motion: All bounce animations are disabled for users who have enabled "Reduce Motion" in their OS accessibility settings.
