Detailed i18n Guidelines

      

        

          
1. RTL Slide Reversals

          

            Slide animations should match the document reading direction.
            For LTR, slide elements left-to-right. For RTL, reverse the translation offset direction to slide right-to-left.
          

        


        

          
2. Max-Width Transitions

          

            Localized translations (like German or Spanish) can be up to 40% longer than English text.
            Avoid hardcoding widths on animated elements to prevent layout reflows.
