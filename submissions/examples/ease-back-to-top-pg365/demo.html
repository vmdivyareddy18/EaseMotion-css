Animated Back to Top

Scroll down to reveal the button in the bottom-right corner, then click it (or press Enter while it's focused) to smoothly return here.

Why a back-to-top button?

Long-form pages and landing pages make users scroll a long way. A back-to-top button gives them a quick, low-friction way to return to the navigation or hero section without repeatedly swiping or scrolling.

How this one behaves

The button stays hidden and non-interactive until the page has been scrolled roughly one viewport height. Once past that threshold it fades and springs into place; scrolling back near the top eases it back out. Clicking it (or activating it via keyboard) smooth-scrolls the page back to the top.
