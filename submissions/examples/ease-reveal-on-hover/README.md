# Reveal on Hover – Hidden content reveals with animation

> A production-ready reveal on hover component where hidden content smoothly reveals with various animations including slide, fade, zoom, rotate, slide-up, and scale-up effects. Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—no dependencies required.

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **6 reveal effects** – Slide, Fade, Zoom, Rotate, Slide Up, Scale Up
- ✅ **Smooth animations** – Spring-like easing transitions
- ✅ **Multiple card types** – Image cards and text cards
- ✅ **Toggle effects** – Cycle through effects
- ✅ **Random effect** – Apply random effect
- ✅ **Delay toggle** – Enable staggered animation delay
- ✅ **Keyboard shortcuts** – Ctrl+E (effect), Ctrl+R (reset), Ctrl+D (delay)
- ✅ **6 variants** – Slide, Fade, Zoom, Rotate, Slide Up, Scale
- ✅ **Responsive** – Adapts to any screen size
- ✅ **Light/Dark mode** – Auto-adjusts to system preference
- ✅ **Zero dependencies** – No libraries or frameworks
