# Omnichannel Customer Intelligence Platform

An omnichannel customer intelligence dashboard built with EaseMotion CSS tokens. Tracks channel performance, customer segments, satisfaction scores, recent interactions, and key intelligence KPIs across 6 channels.

## Features

- **Stat cards** — total customers, active rate, churn rate, satisfaction score
- **Channel performance** — 6 channels (web, mobile, email, chat, social, in-store) with engagement bars and user counts
- **Customer segments** — 6 segments (loyal advocates to churned) with counts and percentages
- **Channel CSAT** — satisfaction scores per channel with color-coded bars
- **Recent interactions** — timestamped feed of customer touchpoints across channels
- **Intelligence KPIs** — LTV, avg order value, NPS, retention rate, response time, cross-channel rate
- **Platform overview** — aggregate data grid (6 channels, 14.2K daily interactions, etc.)
- Responsive 12-column grid with dark mode and reduced motion support
