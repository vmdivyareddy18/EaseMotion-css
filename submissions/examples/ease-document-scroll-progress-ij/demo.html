The document-scroll-progress component wraps a fixed-position progress bar at the top of the viewport and an optional circular indicator badge. Both are driven by a single scroll listener that reads scrollTop and scrollHeight to compute the percentage.

Custom properties let you skin the progress bar: swap the --progress-color to match your brand, adjust --progress-height for a thicker or thinner bar, or change the --indicator-bg to contrast with your theme.

This component is zero-dependency and works in every modern browser. The JavaScript footprint is minimal — just a scroll handler and a few DOM reads.
