Support FAQ

Common Inquiries

How does the skin matching profile work?

After subscribing, you will complete a 2-minute skin consultation survey. You'll specify your skin type (oily, dry, sensitive), skin tone, and cosmetic color preferences. Our sommeliers of skincare customize your package items accordingly!

What clean standards do you enforce?

We ban over 1,500 ingredients, including parabens, sulfates, synthetic fragrances, and phthalates. All items must be organic-certified, cruelty-free, and packaged in recyclable containers.

Can I return a product if it breaks me out?

Yes. Skin sensitivity happens. If a featured product doesn't work for your skin type, let our customer concierge know, and we'll send a complimentary replacement item from our vault in your next box.
