# Product Quick View

## What does this do?
Animated product card quick-view interaction where hovering a product card reveals a quick-view overlay with smooth fade and slide transitions.

## How is it used?
Use `.quick-view-card` on a product card container and `.quick-view-overlay` for the overlay that appears on hover.

## Why is this useful?
Enables quick product previews without navigation, improving browsing efficiency in e-commerce catalog pages.
