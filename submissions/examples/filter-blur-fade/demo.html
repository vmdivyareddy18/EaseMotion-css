Reading Behind Blur

This panel has a backdrop-filter: blur(8px) over a multi-color gradient background. The text remains crisp because backdrop-filter only affects the area behind the element, not the element itself.

The darker semi-transparent background (rgba(10, 15, 30, 0.7)) enhances contrast while the blur softens the underlying pattern.
