Accessibility Fix

Navbar Contrast Optimization

Resolving theme toggle button visibility barriers to meet WCAG AA contrast ratios (minimum 3:1 for UI controls).

🔬 Inspection & Resolution Parameters

  • The Bug: Muted gray icon variables caused the theme toggle switch to bleed completely into dark background layouts.
  • The Optimization: Rebalanced color tokens to enforce a bright, high-contrast text color fill state, paired with interactive focus indicators.

💡 Click the toggle icon in the top right corner of the navbar to observe smooth state switches and high-visibility contrast ratios.
