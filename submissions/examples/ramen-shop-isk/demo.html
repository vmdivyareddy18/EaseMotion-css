Simmered to Perfection

Our recipe begins with pure spring water and selected bones, simmered continuously at a rolling boil to extract every bit of rich collagen. The noodles are handmade daily in our shop, calibrated to the exact humidity of the room to ensure a perfect springy bite (katamen).
