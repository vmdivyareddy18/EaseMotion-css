Curious Parents FAQ

Are the materials safe and non-toxic?

Absolutely. Safety is our single highest priority. All paints, glues, clays, and chemicals are thoroughly lab-tested and comply fully with ASTM D-4236 standards. Everything is child-friendly and water-soluble.

What if my child finds a project too hard?

Our guides are designed for age-appropriate levels, but children develop at different rates. If a project is too challenging or too easy, simply contact our support, and we'll adjust your age group box starting next month!

Can I pause or skip a month for vacation?

Yes! If you're going to be away or need a break, you can pause or skip upcoming shipments inside your online portal with a single click. There are zero reactivation fees or lock-ins.
