# Global Semiconductor Manufacturing Intelligence Platform

**Phase 403** - Cleanroom manufacturing intelligence platform monitoring fabrication lines, yields, and production quality for semiconductor facilities.

## Overview

This comprehensive platform provides real-time monitoring of semiconductor fabrication operations including wafer yield tracking, equipment health monitoring, defect detection, and production scheduling. Manufacturing engineers can visualize cleanroom conditions, optimize lithography processes, and ensure quality standards across all production lines.

## Features

### Fabrication Overview
Real-time dashboard displaying fab yield, running production lines, wafer output, and equipment health metrics with live status indicators.

### Wafer Production
Interactive visualization of wafer manufacturing progress with lot tracking, cycle time analysis, and throughput metrics across all fabrication stages.

### Cleanroom Monitoring
Environmental monitoring of temperature, humidity, and particle counts within cleanroom facilities to maintain optimal manufacturing conditions.

### Equipment Health
Predictive maintenance dashboard showing equipment utilization, MTBF metrics, and maintenance schedules for all fabrication tools.

### Executive Manufacturing Dashboard
High-level KPIs for executive leadership including yield trends, production capacity, and quality metrics for board-ready reporting.
