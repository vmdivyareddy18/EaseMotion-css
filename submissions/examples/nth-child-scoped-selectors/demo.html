How it works

  • .item:nth-child(1 of .visible) — first visible item gets a gold border
  • .item:nth-child(2 of .visible) — second visible item gets a silver border
  • .item:nth-child(3 of .visible) — third visible item gets bronze
  • .item:nth-child(n+4 of .visible) — fourth+ visible items get default
