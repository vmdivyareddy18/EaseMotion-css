# 🔤 Word Scramble

A classic word scramble game where you unscramble letters to form words. Test your vocabulary and speed with timed rounds and increasing word length!

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Click **"Start Game"** or press **Space** to begin
2. **Unscramble** the letters to form a word
3. **Type** your answer or click the letter buttons
4. **Submit** your answer (press Enter or click Submit)
5. **Score points** for each correct answer
6. **Beat the clock** — you have 30 seconds per round!

## ⌨️ Controls

| Action | Input |
|--------|-------|
| Start Game | Click Start or press `Space` |
| Submit Answer | Press `Enter` or click Submit |
| Skip Word | Click Skip or press `S` |
| Reset Game | Press `R` |
| Type Answer | Keyboard input / Letter buttons |

## ✨ Features

- 🔤 **300+ Words** – Increasing difficulty with longer words
- ⏱️ **Timed Rounds** – 30 seconds per round
- 📊 **Score System** – Points based on speed and round bonus
- 💡 **Hints** – Each word has a helpful clue
- 🖱️ **Letter Buttons** – Click or tap to build words
- 📈 **Streak Tracking** – Bonus motivation for consecutive correct answers
- 🏆 **Best Score** – Saved in browser local storage
- 📱 **Responsive Design** – Works on desktop and mobile
- ⌨️ **Keyboard Shortcuts** – Quick access for power users

## 🎯 Scoring

| Factor | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Base Correct Answer | 10 |
| Time Bonus | ½ × Time Remaining |
| Round Bonus | 5 × (Round ÷ 2) |
| Streak Bonus | Accumulates with consecutive correct answers |

## 📊 Difficulty Progression

| Rounds | Word Length |
|--------|-------------|
| 1-2 | 3-4 letters |
| 3-4 | 4-5 letters |
| 5-6 | 5-6 letters |
| 7+ | 7+ letters |
