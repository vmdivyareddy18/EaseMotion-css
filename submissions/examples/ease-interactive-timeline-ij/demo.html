Milestones

2018

Project Founded

The idea was born and the first prototype was built over a weekend hackathon.

What started as a side project quickly gained traction. Within three months the team grew to five core contributors and the first funding round closed.

2019

Public Beta Launch

First public release with support for core features and an early adopter program.

Over 10,000 users signed up in the first week. The feedback loop helped shape the roadmap for the next major iteration.

2020

Series A Funding

Secured $5M in Series A to scale engineering and expand market reach.

With the new funding the team grew to 25 people, the infrastructure was rebuilt for scale, and enterprise features were introduced.

2022

Global Expansion

Opened offices in EMEA and APAC, reaching customers in over 40 countries.

Localisation in 12 languages, regional data centres, and strategic partnerships drove a 3× increase in annual recurring revenue.

2024

AI Platform Launch

Launched AI-powered features that transformed how users interact with the product.

Built on custom LLMs fine-tuned on domain data, the platform achieved a 40% efficiency gain for power users and won "Best Innovation" at TechCrunch Disrupt.
