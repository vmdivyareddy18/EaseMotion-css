# Archerfish

A pure CSS and HTML archerfish taking aim from below the surface and shooting a jet of water to knock a beetle off an overhanging leaf.

## What it shows

- A water jet fired as a volley of droplets on staggered delays, arcing up along a translate path timed to the fish's aim and the bug's fall
- The whole sequence choreographed on one 4s timeline: the fish tilts to aim, the mouth puckers, the jet flies, the bug is struck and tumbles into the water, a splash ring spreads
- Bold black bars painted across the silver flank with hard stop linear gradient bands
- A forked tail and fins on their own fast flutter cycles, under a bright waterline
- No images, no JavaScript, no external dependencies

## Usage

Open `demo.html` in any modern browser.

## Accessibility

All motion stops under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.
