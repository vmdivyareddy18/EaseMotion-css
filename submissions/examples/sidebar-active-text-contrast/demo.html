What changed

The active sidebar item now uses a darker text color with a stronger accent while keeping the light purple background. This preserves the design language and improves legibility for users in bright themes.

Why it matters

Low-contrast active text can disappear in a documentation sidebar. A clearer active state helps users quickly identify their current section without relying on hover or extra cues.
