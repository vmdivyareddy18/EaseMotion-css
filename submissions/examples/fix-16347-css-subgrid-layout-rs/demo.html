CSS Subgrid Utilities

Align internal elements of different cards (like headers, bodies, and footers) perfectly across a grid row using `grid-template-rows: subgrid`. Without subgrid, cards with different amounts of content would be misaligned internally.

This card has a massive amount of content.

It just keeps going and going. In a normal flexbox or grid setup without subgrid, the footer button for this card would be pushed way down, while the footer buttons for the other cards would sit much higher up.

Thanks to subgrid, the buttons on all three cards align perfectly at the bottom of the row!
