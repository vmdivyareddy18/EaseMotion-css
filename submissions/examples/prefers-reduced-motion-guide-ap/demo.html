Understanding Vestibular Conditions

      

        

          
What is Vestibular Disorder?

          

            The vestibular system controls balance, tracking, and spatial orientation. Users with vestibular disorders
            can experience severe vertigo, nausea, headaches, and physical disorientation when viewing motion on a screen,
            especially parallax offsets or rapid zoom scales.
          

        


        

          
The Value of Subtle Motion

          

            Disabling animations entirely can degrade page transition feedback. Applying **Subtle Motion**
            (such as replacing translations/movement offsets with simple opacity-based cross-fades) preserves
            interaction states while protecting user comfort.
