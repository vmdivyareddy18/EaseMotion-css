Before You Buy

Sizing & Condition Guide

We grade every item before it reaches our shelves so you know exactly what you're getting.

⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Excellent Vintage

As close to unworn as vintage gets. No flaws, no fading, no repairs. Original hardware, labels, and lining intact. Cleaned and ready to wear.

Premium Pick
⭐⭐⭐⭐

Very Good

Light signs of wear consistent with age. Minor softening, slight fading in low-visibility areas. No damage, stains, or structural issues.

Best Value
⭐⭐⭐

Good

Visible character marks — small fading, gentle wear at cuffs or collar. All noted in full in the listing. Structurally sound and fully functional.

Character Piece
📏

Measurements Guide

Vintage sizing runs differently from modern cuts. Every item includes flat-lay bust, waist, hip, and length measurements — always measure, never assume. Email us if you're unsure.

How We Source

Every Find Has a Story Worth Telling

We travel to over twelve markets every month across the Northeast — from antique barns in Vermont to estate sales in Connecticut and flea markets in Brooklyn. Nothing is bought in bulk. Every piece is held, examined, tried on.

  • 🤲
    Handpicked Collections

    Individually selected by our founder, not a buying algorithm.

  • ♻️
    Sustainable Fashion

    Every preloved piece prevents a new one from being manufactured.

  • 🌾
    Local Markets & Estate Sales

    We support small-town sellers and keep money in local communities.

  • 🔍
    Quality Inspection

    Every item is cleaned, repaired if needed, and graded before listing.
