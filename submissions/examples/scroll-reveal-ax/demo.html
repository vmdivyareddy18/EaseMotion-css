Scroll Reveal Animations

Each section appears with a different animation when scrolled into view.

⬆️ Fade Up Reveal

This card slides up and fades in when it enters the viewport.

⬇️ Fade Down Reveal

This card slides down and fades in when scrolled into view.

⬅️ Slide from Left

This card slides in from the left with a fade effect.

➡️ Slide from Right

This card slides in from the right and fades in.

🔍 Zoom Reveal

This card scales up from a smaller size and fades in.

Uses IntersectionObserver + EaseMotion classes. No custom keyframes.
