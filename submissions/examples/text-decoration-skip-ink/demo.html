Text Decoration Skip Ink Utility Classes

Controls whether underline/overline/line-through decorations skip over descenders (g, j, p, q, y). Use words with descenders below to see the difference — the gap under the loops of each letter.

💡 Note: Supported in all modern browsers. Safari has historically defaulted to skipping ink even without this property set; Chrome and Firefox respect auto as skip-by-default too, so .ease-skip-ink-none is the one with the most visible effect.

Common use cases

  • Body links and inline text — .ease-skip-ink-auto (default, safest)
  • Decorative underlines, buttons, brand wordmarks — .ease-skip-ink-none
  • Long-form article links with heavy descender usage — .ease-skip-ink-all
