# Flying Fish

A pure CSS and HTML flying fish breaking the surface and gliding a long arc over the swell on outstretched pectoral wings.

## What it shows

- A launch and glide built from two stacked animations on nested elements: one carries the fish along its arc, the other pitches it nose up then nose down as the arc flattens
- Wings and pelvic fins from clip-path vanes with a fine repeating linear gradient for the rays
- The tail sculling hard only during the first moments after launch, then going still for the glide
- Spray flicked off the surface at the moment of take off, and a swelling sea below
- No images, no JavaScript, no external dependencies

## Usage

Open `demo.html` in any modern browser.

## Accessibility

All motion stops under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.
