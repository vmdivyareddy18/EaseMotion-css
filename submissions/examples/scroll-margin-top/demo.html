scroll-margin-top

Click the nav links — each section has a different scroll-margin-top value, affecting how much space appears between the fixed header and the section.

scroll-margin-top: 0px

The section snaps flush to the top of the viewport. Content is hidden behind the fixed header.

scroll-margin-top: 40px

A 40px offset creates a small gap between the header and the section content.

scroll-margin-top: 80px

With 80px, the section is clearly separated from the header, matching the fixed header height.

scroll-margin-top: 120px

A generous 120px margin gives the section breathing room below the navigation bar.

scroll-margin-top: 160px

The largest offset — the section appears well below the header, with plenty of whitespace.

Each section's scroll-margin-top is set inline. Open DevTools to inspect and modify the values.
