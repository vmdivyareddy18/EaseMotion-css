# Online Therapy Platform Template
A soothing, highly professional, and security-focused template for online therapy platforms, mental health counseling apps, and telehealth wellbeing services.

- **Design**: Uses a "Calm-Peace" palette with indigo primary headers, teal support accents, and soft, clean layouts to communicate empathy, safety, and professional reliability.
- **Use Case**: Mental health platforms, virtual counseling services, and emotional wellness apps.
