How to Use

To integrate these buttons, add the HTML snippets below to your layout. Link the stylesheet `style.css` and use a simple click trigger script to toggle the active `.ease-active` class. For path morphing (like Play to Stop or Plus to Check), ensure that the SVG <path> elements contain the exact same number and order of coordinate points to enable a clean morphing transition.

HTML Markup Structures

<!-- Toggle 1: Hamburger to Close -->
<button class="ease-morph-btn ease-morph-hamburger">
  <svg viewBox="0 0 24 24">
    <line class="line-top" x1="4" y1="6" x2="20" y2="6"></line>
    <line class="line-middle" x1="4" y1="12" x2="20" y2="12"></line>
    <line class="line-bottom" x1="4" y1="18" x2="20" y2="18"></line>
  </svg>
</button>

<!-- Toggle 2: Play to Stop -->
<button class="ease-morph-btn ease-morph-play">
  <svg viewBox="0 0 24 24">
    <path></path>
  </svg>
</button>

<!-- Toggle 3: Plus to Check -->
<button class="ease-morph-btn ease-morph-plus">
  <svg viewBox="0 0 24 24">
    <path class="line-vertical"></path>
    <path class="line-horizontal"></path>
  </svg>
</button>
