Technical Breakdown

Understanding the three key ingredients behind the tilt effect.

Perspective

The parent container gets perspective: 1000px to create a 3D space. This determines the depth and intensity of the tilt. Lower values (e.g. 500px) create a more dramatic effect.

RotateY

On hover, the card applies rotateY(var(--ease-tilt-angle)). The positive angle tilts the card to the left in 3D space, while transform-origin: center keeps the pivot centered.

CSS Variable

The tilt angle is controlled by --ease-tilt-angle with a default of 8deg. Override this on any card to change its tilt independently.
