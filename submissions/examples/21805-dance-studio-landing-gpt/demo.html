FAQ

Helpful answers for first-time students and parents.

Do beginners need prior dance experience?

No. We assess comfort, coordination, and goals during the trial and place students in the right level.

What should I wear to a trial class?

Wear comfortable movement clothing, tie back long hair, and bring socks or clean indoor footwear.

Are there separate batches for children and adults?

Yes. Kids, teens, adults, and advanced performance teams follow dedicated schedules and age-appropriate instruction.

How often do students perform on stage?

Members can join the annual showcase, seasonal in-studio presentations, and selected external competition teams.
