Accessibility Guidance (WCAG 2.1)

      

        

          
Success Criterion 1.4.10

          
Reflow & Wrapping

          

            When utilizing hardcoded basis scales (like `.flex-basis-64` or
            `.flex-basis-96`), ensure the flex container supports `flex-wrap:
            wrap;` or includes responsive modifiers (e.g.
            `.sm-flex-basis-full`). This guarantees content reflows vertically
            on smaller viewports without introducing horizontal scrollbars.
          

        


        

          
Focus Order

          
Logical Tab Sequence (2.4.3)

          

            Avoid shifting flex ordering parameters (`order-1`, `order-first`)
            extensively alongside flex-basis changes, as this can disconnect
            visual tab focus from the underlying DOM layout flow. Maintain
            matching focus order.
