🛠️ Implementing the Fix

Because EaseMotion's CSS transitions operate strictly on the targeted element rather than wrapping them automatically, apply overflow: hidden on the element's parent container or section wrapper to ensure layouts remain clean.

<!-- ✅ Clean wrapper prevents scrollbar flicker -->
<section class="hero-section" style="overflow: hidden; position: relative;">
  <div class="hero-bg ease-zoom-in">
    <!-- Hero background content -->
  </div>
</section>
