# ⌨️ Typing Speedrun

Test your typing skills in this fast-paced word-typing game! Type words before they reach the bottom, battle bosses, and track your WPM and accuracy.

## 🎮 How to Play

- **Type words** as they appear on screen
- **Press Enter** or **Space** to submit your typed word
- **Eliminate words** before they reach the bottom
- **Defeat bosses** by typing their words
- **Track your speed** with WPM and accuracy stats

## ✨ Features

- **⌨️ Real-time Typing** - Type words before they fall
- **⚡ WPM & Accuracy** - Track your performance
- **🐉 Boss Battles** - Defeat bosses by typing their words
- **🎨 Color-coded Words** - Each word has a unique color
- **📈 Progressive Difficulty** - Words get faster over time
- **🎯 3 Difficulty Levels** - Easy, Medium, Hard
- **🏆 Score System** - Points for speed and accuracy

## 🎯 Difficulty Levels

| Level | Timer | Word Speed | Boss Health |
|-------|-------|------------|-------------|
| 😊 Easy | 35s | Slow | 50 |
| 🤔 Medium | 25s | Medium | 60+ |
| 😈 Hard | 15s | Fast | 70+ |

## 🏆 Scoring

| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Correct Word | 10 + (speed × 10) |
| Boss Defeat | 100 |
| Accuracy Bonus | Bonus for high accuracy |

## 🎮 Controls

| Action | Input |
|--------|-------|
| Type Word | Keyboard typing |
| Submit Word | Enter / Space |
| Start/Restart | Click button |
| Escape | End game early |

## 💡 Tips & Strategies

1. **Focus on Accuracy** - Wrong words hurt your score
2. **Prioritize Low Words** - Type words closest to the bottom first
3. **Boss Battles** - Type the boss's word letters in order
4. **Practice Regularly** - Speed improves with practice
5. **Start Easy** - Build confidence on lower difficulty
