How it works

  • @media (dynamic-range: high) targets HDR-capable displays
  • color-gamut: p3 detects wide-color support
  • Wide-gamut oklch() colors with sRGB fallbacks outside the query
  • HDR colors include box-shadow glow for enhanced luminance
