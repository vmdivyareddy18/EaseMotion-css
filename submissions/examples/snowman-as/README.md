# Snowman

### What does this do?

It shows a snowman standing in falling snow. Three stacked snowballs make the body, topped with a hat, coal eyes and buttons, a carrot nose, a stick smile, and twig arms. The whole snowman sways gently while snowflakes drift down past him and settle on the ground. Under reduced motion the snow and sway both stop.

### How is it used?

```html
<div class="snowman">
  <div class="head"></div>
  <div class="mid"></div>
  <div class="base"></div>
</div>
```

The three body sections are overlapping radial-gradient circles for a soft, round look, the face details are small positioned shapes, and the arms are rotated bars with a branching twig on each. Snowflakes fall on staggered durations and delays, and the `sway` animation rocks the snowman a couple of degrees.

### Why is it useful?

Winter themes, holiday cards, and seasonal loaders use a snowman. This builds a swaying snowman in falling snow with pure CSS shapes and animation, no images, with a reduced motion fallback.
