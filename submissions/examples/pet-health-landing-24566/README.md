# Pet Health Tracker Template
A friendly, warm, and highly visual landing page template for pet care applications, veterinary wellness tools, and pet tracking software.

- **Design**: Uses a "Companion-Care" palette with soft-cream backgrounds, paw-teal trust accents, and heart-coral action buttons to convey love, reliability, and ease of use.
- **Use Case**: Pet wellness apps, veterinary practice support tools, and consumer pet tracking platforms.
