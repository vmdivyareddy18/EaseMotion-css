Implementation Notes

Pure CSS

No JavaScript is required for shimmer accents and timeline structure.

Accessible

Includes skip link, clear focus ring, and readable text contrast.

Responsive

Switches to a single-column rail with balanced spacing on smaller screens.

EaseMotion classes

Demo includes ease-fade-in, ease-slide-up, and ease-hover-grow classes.
