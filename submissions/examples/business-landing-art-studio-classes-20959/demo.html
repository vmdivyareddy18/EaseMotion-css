What we teach

Expert-led courses designed for total beginners to advanced practitioners.

Painting & Drawing

Master the fundamentals of composition, color theory, and technique using acrylics, oils, or charcoal.

  • Intro to Acrylics
  • Life Drawing
  • Plein Air Workshops

Ceramics & Sculpture

  • Wheel Throwing 101
  • Hand-building Basics
  • Advanced Glazing

Printmaking

Explore the graphic art of printmaking through linocut, screen printing, and monotype methods.

  • Screen Printing Basics
  • Linocut Relief
  • Textile Printing
