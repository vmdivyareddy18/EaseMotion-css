## Thunderstorm Animation

A dark storm scene with jagged lightning bolts that illuminate the sky in quick flashes. Rain streaks fall diagonally at varying speeds for a realistic storm effect.

**Features:**
- Forked lightning bolt with branches via pseudo-elements
- Bright flash illumination on the storm sky
- Multiple rain streaks at staggered timings
- Respects `prefers-reduced-motion`
