Frequently Asked Questions

What credit score do I need?

Most conventional loans require 620+. FHA loans may accept 580+. Our advisors can help find the best fit for your profile.

How long does closing take?

Average is 30 days. With our digital platform, many borrowers close in as few as 21 days.

Are there prepayment penalties?

Never. All TrustMark loans come with zero prepayment penalties — pay off your loan early with no fees.

Do you offer VA or USDA loans?

Yes — we offer VA loans with 0% down for qualified veterans and USDA rural housing loans with competitive rates.

What documents do I need?

Typically W‑2s, pay stubs, bank statements, and tax returns from the last two years. We'll guide you through the checklist.
