From Accountant to Comedian

Marcus Bell spent four years crunching numbers for a Fortune 500 company before realising that the funniest thing about spreadsheets was that people took them seriously.

His debut special "Unqualified Opinions" on Netflix hit 14 million views in its first week. His second special "Still Not Sorry" won the Edinburgh Comedy Award in 2023.

When he is not on stage, he is either at a buffet, arguing with the algorithm, or trying to teach his cat basic boundaries.
