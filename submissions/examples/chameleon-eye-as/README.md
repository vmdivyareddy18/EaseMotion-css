# Chameleon Eye

A pure CSS and HTML chameleon on a branch, its turret eye swivelling independently before its tongue shoots out to snatch a fly.

## What it shows

- A domed turret eye that rotates on its own timing, the conical lid a masked repeating conic gradient with the pupil peeping through
- A ballistic tongue that fires out with a big scaleX from a hinge at the mouth, tipped with a sticky pad, timed to the gape and the fly's freeze
- A prehensile tail from four shrinking discs that curl together
- Slow colour shifting skin driven by an animated hue-rotate filter over the whole animal
- No images, no JavaScript, no external dependencies

## Usage

Open `demo.html` in any modern browser.

## Accessibility

All motion stops under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.
