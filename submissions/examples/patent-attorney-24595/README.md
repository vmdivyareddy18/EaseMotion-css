# Patent Attorney Template
A sharp, precise, and tech-forward template for patent attorneys, intellectual property firms, and innovation-driven legal consultancies.

- **Design**: Uses an "Innovation-Authority" palette with intellect-charcoal deep grays, innovation-blue highlight lines, and blueprint-inspired backgrounds to convey technical rigor, security, and strategic foresight.
- **Use Case**: Patent law firms, IP-focused consultancies, and technology-oriented legal practices.
