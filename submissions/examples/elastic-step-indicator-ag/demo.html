Clinical Onboarding

Patient registration and workflow verification pipeline

1. Identity Verification

Please upload your insurance card and government-issued ID to initialize the health coverage audit.

2. Symptom Log

Outline current symptoms, allergies, and medication history for review by the clinical panel.

3. Choose Practitioner

Select your assigned telehealth professional or choose from available primary care physicians.

4. Booking Details

Select a time and date slot for your primary diagnostic evaluation and video session.

5. Registration Finalized

Verify details, complete the clinical copay form, and schedule your appointment sequence.
