📚 What is Expandable Card?

An expandable card is a UI component that reveals additional content when clicked. It's perfect for:

  • ✅ FAQ sections
  • ✅ Collapsible panels
  • ✅ Accordion menus
  • ✅ Progressive disclosure

The smooth height transition creates a polished, professional feel.

🎨 Customization Options

This component is highly customizable:

  • 🎨 Colors: Match your brand
  • 📏 Size: Adjustable padding and fonts
  • ⏱️ Animation: Control speed and easing
  • 🔄 Behavior: Accordion or independent
  • 🎯 Icons: Custom toggle icons
