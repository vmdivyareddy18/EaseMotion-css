Executive Chef

Julian Vance

With over two decades spent in the kitchens of Paris and Kyoto, Chef Julian Vance brings a philosophy of minimalist perfection to Lumière.

His approach strips away the unnecessary, allowing the innate brilliance of heritage ingredients to take center stage. Every plate is an expression of deep respect for the farmers, foragers, and fishermen who make our menu possible.
