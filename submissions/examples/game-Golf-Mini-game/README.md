# ⛳ Golf Mini-Game

A physics-based golf mini-game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Aim, charge your shot, and navigate obstacles to sink the ball in the fewest strokes possible!

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Click **"Start Game"** to begin your round
2. **Aim** by moving your mouse/touch near the ball
3. **Charge power** by clicking and holding on the ball
4. **Release** to swing and launch the ball
5. **Navigate** sand traps and water hazards
6. **Sink the ball** in the hole to complete the hole
7. **Track your score** against par!

## ⛳ Features

- 🎯 **Physics-Based Ball Movement** – Realistic ball physics with gravity, friction, and wind
- 💨 **Wind System** – Affects ball trajectory
- 🏖️ **Obstacles** – Sand traps (slow) and water hazards (penalty)
- 🏌️ **Power Meter** – Charge your shot strength
- 📊 **Score Tracking** – Strokes, par, and total score
- 🏆 **Best Score** – Saved in browser local storage
- 🎨 **Beautiful Visuals** – Detailed course with clouds, grass, and obstacles
- 📱 **Responsive Design** – Works on desktop and mobile
- 🖱️ **Intuitive Controls** – Click and drag to aim and shoot

## ⌨️ Controls

| Action | Input |
|--------|-------|
| Aim | Move mouse near ball |
| Charge Power | Click and hold on ball |
| Swing | Release mouse/touch |
| Start Game | Click Start or press `Space` |
| Next Hole | Press `Space` or `N` |
| Reset Game | Press `R` |

## 🎯 Scoring

| Result | Score |
|--------|-------|
| Hole-in-one | -2+ |
| Eagle | -2 |
| Birdie | -1 |
| Par | 0 |
| Bogey | +1 |
| Double Bogey | +2+ |
