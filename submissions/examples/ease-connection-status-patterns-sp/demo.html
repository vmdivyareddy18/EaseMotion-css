Motion guidance

Animation mapping

  • Online — .ease-pulse on indicator dot (subtle alive signal)
  • Offline — .ease-shake on badge (attention grab)
  • Reconnecting — .ease-pulse (retry in progress)
  • Slow — .ease-pulse + .ease-fade-in on card

ARIA roles

  • role="status" for online / reconnecting / slow
  • role="alert" for offline (interrupts screen readers)
  • aria-live="polite" on reconnecting updates

Production tips

  • Listen to navigator.onLine + online/offline events.
  • Cap pulse loops with --ease-animation-iterations: 3.
  • Respect prefers-reduced-motion — disable shake/pulse.
