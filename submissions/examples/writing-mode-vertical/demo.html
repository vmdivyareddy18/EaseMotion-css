writing-mode & text-orientation

Vertical Japanese-style text layout with mixed horizontal/vertical content.

writing-mode: vertical-rl

Text flows vertically from right to left (traditional East Asian layout).

日本語の文章は縦書きで表示されます。右から左へと流れます。

CSS allows mixing vertical and horizontal text seamlessly.

writing-mode: vertical-lr

Text flows vertically from left to right (Mongolian-style layout).

Vertical left-to-right is used for certain scripts.

text-orientation: upright

Latin characters and digits are rendered upright instead of rotated.
