# River Cruise Company Template
A serene, navigational template tailored for luxury river cruise lines, boutique travel operators, and waterway tour agencies.

- **Design**: Uses an "Aqua & Mist" palette with deep river-blues, clean mist-greys, and warm wood accents to evoke smooth travel, serenity, and professional maritime standards.
- **Use Case**: River cruise lines, canal tour companies, and regional waterway travel agencies.
