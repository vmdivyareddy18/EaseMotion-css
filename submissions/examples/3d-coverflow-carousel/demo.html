How to Use

Simply add the markup snippet below to your HTML, import the stylesheet `style.css`, and initialize the active index toggles. Adjacent cards will transition using smooth, hardware-accelerated 3D translations and bounces.

HTML Markup Structure

<div class="ease-coverflow">
  <div class="ease-coverflow-track">
    <div class="ease-coverflow-card ease-prev-2">
      <div class="ease-coverflow-card-content">
        <!-- Content Here -->
      </div>
    </div>
    <div class="ease-coverflow-card ease-prev-1">
      <!-- Content Here -->
    </div>
    <div class="ease-coverflow-card ease-active">
      <!-- Content Here -->
    </div>
    <div class="ease-coverflow-card ease-next-1">
      <!-- Content Here -->
    </div>
    <div class="ease-coverflow-card ease-next-2">
      <!-- Content Here -->
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
