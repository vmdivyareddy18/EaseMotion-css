CSS background-size Showcase

Demonstrating auto, cover, contain, and custom sizing — all with smooth EaseMotion-style transitions.

auto

The image keeps its original (intrinsic) size. May overflow or leave empty space.

background-size: auto;

cover

The image scales to fill the container completely. Parts may be cropped to preserve aspect ratio.

background-size: cover;

contain

The image scales to fit entirely within the container. Empty space may appear around it.

background-size: contain;

custom (50% 50%)

You can supply explicit width and height values — pixels, percentages, or units of your choice.

background-size: 50% 50%;

stretch (100% 100%)

The image is forced to fill the container exactly, distorting the aspect ratio if needed.

background-size: 100% 100%;

hover zoom

Hover over the visual to see background-size animate smoothly from cover to a larger zoom — perfect for hero sections.

transition: background-size 0.6s ease;

All visuals use inline SVG data URIs — no external assets, no CDN, no JavaScript.
