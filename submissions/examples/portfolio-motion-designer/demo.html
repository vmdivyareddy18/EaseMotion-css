/ 02 — How I Work

A process built
for great motion.

Every project follows a deliberate five-step rhythm — from the first conversation to the final deliverable, with you in the room at every milestone.

  1. 01

    Discovery

    We start with the why. Brand audits, mood boards, references — building a shared language before a single frame is animated.

  2. 02

    Storyboarding

    Frame-by-frame planning. Pacing, transitions and key beats are locked in before production — no surprises later.

  3. 03

    Animation

    Where the magic happens. 2D, 3D, simulations and compositing come together in collaborative review cycles.

  4. 04

    Sound Design

    Motion without sound is half the story. Custom score, foley and mix — delivered alongside the picture.

  5. 05

    Delivery

    Final masters in every format you need — broadcast, social, web — plus a documented motion guide for future teams.
