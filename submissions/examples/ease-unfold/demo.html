ease-unfold

An entrance animation that scales an element open from scaleY(0) to scaleY(1), like a drawer or blind unfolding downward. Click a drawer's header to open or close it, or use the staggered demo below to unfold several at once.

A lightweight, dependency-free animation library you drop into any project with a single class name — no build step required.
Most animations are pure CSS. A small amount of JS is only used here to measure real heights and toggle classes on click.
Yes — pair .ease-unfold with .ease-unfold-delay-1/2/3 to stagger several drawers opening in sequence.
