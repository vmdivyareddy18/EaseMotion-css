# E-Commerce Product Page

## What does this do?
A complete e-commerce product listing page with filterable sidebar, product grid, featured product section, customer reviews, newsletter signup, and pagination.

## How is it used?
```html
<div class="pp-layout">
  <aside class="pp-sidebar">Filters</aside>
  <main class="pp-main">
    <div class="pp-grid">
      <div class="pp-card">Product Card</div>
    </div>
  </main>
</div>
```

## Why is it useful?
Provides a full-featured product browsing experience for e-commerce sites. Includes category filters, price range, brand filtering, star ratings, pagination, featured product spotlight, customer reviews with helpfulness voting, and newsletter subscription. Responsive grid layout with smooth hover interactions.
