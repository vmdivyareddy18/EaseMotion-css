Face shape guide

Find the frame that sharpens your angles and softens your light.

Our stylists map temple width, brow line, and cheek balance to recommend silhouettes that feel custom without overthinking it.

Round Face

Go for angular rectangles and slim aviators to create length and definition.

Best match: Noir Current

Square Face

Rounded lenses and soft ovals balance strong jawlines with an effortless resort vibe.

Best match: Dune Halo

Heart Face

Navigator and teardrop shapes complement broader brows and refine the lower face.

Best match: Mirage Chrome

Oval Face

Most silhouettes work here, so play with statement proportions and mirrored finishes.

Best match: Azure Drift
