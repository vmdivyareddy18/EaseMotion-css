# Music Box Ballerina

A pure CSS and HTML music box: lift the lid and the ballerina turns on her platter in front of the mirror while the winding key rotates and notes drift out.

## What it shows

- The ballerina and her platter spinning together on rotateY, so she turns to face away and back like the real thing
- A layered tutu from a repeating conic gradient masked into a ring, flaring as she turns
- The lid and its mirror hinged open with a shared rotation, the winding key counter rotating on the case
- A posed figure in arabesque built from rounded segments, bobbing as she spins
- No images, no JavaScript, no external dependencies

## Usage

Open `demo.html` in any modern browser.

## Accessibility

All motion stops under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.
