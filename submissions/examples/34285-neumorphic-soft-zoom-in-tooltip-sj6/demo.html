Evening Wind-Down

Hover a control — or Tab to it — and its explanation card breathes in with a gentle zoom, floating just above the surface.

Dim Lights Fades every connected lamp to 20% warmth over five minutes. Rain Sounds Plays a soft rain loop in the bedroom speakers, off at midnight. Cool Air Drops the thermostat two degrees for deeper sleep cycles. Secure Home Locks both doors and arms the porch sensor until sunrise.
