Readability guidance

Typography tips

  • Use dyslexia-friendly fonts like Lexend or Atkinson Hyperlegible.
  • Increase line-height to 1.7–2.0 for body text.
  • Add slight letter-spacing (0.03–0.06em) and word spacing.
  • Keep line length around 55–65ch — not too wide.

Motion tips

  • Prefer opacity fades over bounce and sharp scale effects.
  • Avoid staggered slide entrances on dense lists.
  • Use slower durations (0.7–1s) with ease, not bounce easing.
  • Never combine tight text with aggressive looping animations.

EaseMotion checklist

  • Pair .ease-fade-in with readable typography.
  • Skip .ease-bounce on primary reading surfaces.
  • Test with prefers-reduced-motion toggle above.
  • Use .ease-card-shadow for structure, not motion overload.
