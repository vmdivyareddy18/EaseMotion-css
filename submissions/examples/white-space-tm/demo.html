White Space

Sets how whitespace inside an element is handled, affecting wrapping and line breaking.

CSS Property: white-space

Variants Demo

white-space: normal White Space variant
white-space: nowrap White Space variant
white-space: pre White Space variant
white-space: pre-wrap White Space variant
white-space: pre-line White Space variant
nowrap
This line extends beyond its container and does not wrap to the next line.
pre
Line one here indented text another line
pre-line
Line one not indented another line

Usage

<div class="white-space">Content here</div>
