# Drag to Reveal – Drag slider to reveal hidden image

> A production-ready drag to reveal component where users drag a slider handle to reveal hidden content with smooth, interactive controls and customizable styling. Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—no dependencies required.

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Drag to reveal** – Interactive slider reveals hidden content
- ✅ **Mouse & touch support** – Works on all devices
- ✅ **Real-time feedback** – Live percentage display
- ✅ **Auto-reveal** – Automatically completes when enabled
- ✅ **Animate reveal** – Auto-animate to 100%
- ✅ **Swap images** – Toggle before/after content
- ✅ **Keyboard shortcuts** – Space (animate), R (reset), Arrow keys
- ✅ **Customizable** – Handle color
- ✅ **4 color variants** – Ocean, Sunset, Gold, Dream
- ✅ **Responsive** – Adapts to any screen size
- ✅ **Light/Dark mode** – Auto-adjusts to system preference
- ✅ **Zero dependencies** – No libraries or frameworks
