## Hurricane Eye Animation

A satellite-view hurricane with concentric spiral bands rotating around a calm central eye. Multiple bands spin at different speeds and directions for a realistic cyclonic effect.

**Features:**
- Central eye with blue radial gradient
- Multiple spiral rain bands with conic gradients
- Alternating spin directions for bands
- Respects `prefers-reduced-motion`
