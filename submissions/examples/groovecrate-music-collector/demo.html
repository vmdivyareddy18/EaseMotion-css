Quality Assurance

Goldmine Grading Standard

We strictly adhere to the international Goldmine standard for grading all vintage records. Buy with confidence knowing exactly what you are putting on your turntable.

Read Full Policy

M / NM

Near Mint

Nearly perfect. Glossy vinyl, no visible scuffs, plays without any surface noise.

VG+

Very Good Plus

Minor signs of wear. Slight cosmetic scuffs that do not affect the listening experience.

VG

Very Good

Evident groove wear. Plays through with light crackle, typically during quiet passages.

G+

Good Plus

Significant wear. Plays through without skipping, but surface noise will be noticeable.
