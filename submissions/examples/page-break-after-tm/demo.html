Page Break After

Forces or avoids a page break after an element when printing.

CSS Property: page-break-after

Variants Demo

page-break-after: auto Page Break After variant
page-break-after: always Page Break After variant
page-break-after: avoid Page Break After variant
page-break-after: left Page Break After variant
page-break-after: right Page Break After variant

Usage

<div class="page-break-after">Content here</div>
