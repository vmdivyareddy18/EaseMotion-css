# ⭐ Ninja Stars

Become a ninja master! Throw shurikens at moving targets, build combos, deflect incoming projectiles, and collect stars in this fast-paced aiming game.

## 🎮 How to Play

- **Click & drag** to aim your shuriken
- **Release** to throw at targets
- **Hit targets** to build your combo
- **Deflect projectiles** for bonus points
- **Collect stars** for extra points
- **Build combos** for higher multipliers!

## ✨ Features

- **🎯 Aim & Throw** - Drag to aim, release to throw
- **🔥 Combo System** - Chain hits for multipliers
- **⭐ Multipliers** - Every 5 combo = +1x multiplier
- **💥 Deflection** - Throw at enemy projectiles to deflect
- **👾 Boss Targets** - Bigger targets with health bars
- **⭐ Star Collectibles** - Collect for bonus points
- **📈 Progressive Difficulty** - Gets harder as you score
- **🎨 Visual Effects** - Trail effects, explosions, particles

## 🎯 Scoring System

| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Normal Hit | 10 × Multiplier |
| Boss Hit | 50 × Multiplier |
| Deflect Projectile | 20 × Multiplier |
| Collect Star | 30 |

## ⭐ Multiplier System

| Combo | Multiplier |
|-------|------------|
| 0-4 | 1x |
| 5-9 | 2x |
| 10-14 | 3x |
| 15-19 | 4x |
| 20+ | 5x+ |

## 🎮 Controls

| Action | Desktop | Mobile |
|--------|---------|--------|
| Aim | Click & drag | Touch & drag |
| Throw | Release | Release |
| Start/Restart | Click button | Tap button |
| Throw (Keyboard) | Space | - |

## 🎯 Target Types

| Type | Health | Points | Visual |
|------|--------|--------|--------|
| Normal | 1 hit | 10× | Gold rings |
| Boss | 3 hits | 50× | Red rings + health bar |

## 💡 Tips & Strategies

1. **Aim Ahead** - Lead your targets for better accuracy
2. **Build Combos** - Chain hits for higher multipliers
3. **Deflect Projectiles** - They give good combo bonuses
4. **Collect Stars** - Appear after defeating targets
5. **Prioritize Bosses** - They give the most points
6. **Don't Rush** - Aim carefully for consistent hits
