Without isolation, internal component blending modes mix with the colorful page backdrop. Enabling ease-isolate boundaries confines the blending context strictly inside the component card.

3D Stacking Context Layering

A stacking context limits z-index hierarchy. When isolated, the purple child (z-index: 10) cannot bleed through and overlap the pink sibling (z-index: 2) which is placed outside the parent.

Parent Container isolation: isolate (z-index: 1)
Child A z-index: 10
Sibling B z-index: 2 (Outside parent)
Apply .ease-isolate:
Stage View:
Status: Parent Container is Isolated. Sibling B (z-index: 2) stacks on top of Parent's internal hierarchy (Child A has z-index: 10 but is trapped inside the parent).
