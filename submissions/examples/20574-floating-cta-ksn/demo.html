Section 1: Engaging Your Users

As users scroll down your page, their attention shifts from the initial headline to your core content. Presenting a persistent floating Call-To-Action right as they get deep into your text increases click-through rates by maintaining visibility without interrupting their reading experience.

Section 2: Smooth Scrolling Interactions

This floating button is powered entirely by native CSS scroll-driven timelines. Rather than using resource-heavy JavaScript window-scroll event listeners, it binds its scale and opacity transforms directly to the document scroll height, running smoothly at 60fps.
