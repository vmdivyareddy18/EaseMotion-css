# Bowerbird

A pure CSS and HTML satin bowerbird arranging blue treasures in the avenue of his bower.

## What it shows

- Twin twig walls woven from a multi stop repeating linear gradient, masked to fray at the top and skewed toward each other to form the avenue
- The bird hopping, bowing and releasing a blue berry that drops onto the collection, all timed on one cycle
- Iridescent satin plumage from a violet blue sheen layer driven by an animated hue-rotate
- A scatter of blue trinkets laid out on the cleared floor
- No images, no JavaScript, no external dependencies

## Usage

Open `demo.html` in any modern browser.

## Accessibility

All motion stops under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.
