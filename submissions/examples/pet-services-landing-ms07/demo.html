Your Trainer

Maya Rodriguez, CPDT-KA

Maya spent a decade studying animal cognition at the University of Illinois before opening Paws & Progress in 2013. She has trained service dogs, therapy dogs, shelter dogs awaiting adoption, and beloved family pets — all with the same core philosophy: build trust first, then build behaviour.

"Dogs don't misbehave out of spite. They do the best they can with the information they have. My job is to give them better information."

🎓
CPDT-KA (Certified Professional Dog Trainer) Certified by the Certification Council for Professional Dog Trainers
🐾
Fear Free Certified Professional (FFCP) Specialised training in low-stress handling and behaviour modification
🏅
AKC Canine Good Citizen Evaluator Official evaluator for the AKC's community-standard certification
📚
B.Sc. Animal Behaviour — University of Illinois, 2011 Studied canine cognition, learning theory, and applied ethology
