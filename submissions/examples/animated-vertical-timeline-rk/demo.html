Animated Vertical Timeline

A responsive, alternating vertical timeline with scroll-reveal animation and hover elevation on each entry.

Project milestones timeline

  1. Jan 2024

    Project Kickoff

    Initial planning phase, defining scope, goals, and the core design language for the project.

  2. Mar 2024

    Design System Complete

    Finalized component library, typography scale, and color tokens used across the whole interface.

  3. Jun 2024

    Beta Launch

    Released to a limited group of testers to gather feedback on usability and performance.

  4. Sep 2024

    Public Release

    Opened up to everyone, with full documentation and a refreshed onboarding flow.

  5. Feb 2025

    v2.0 Milestone

    Major version update introducing new animation utilities and improved accessibility support.
