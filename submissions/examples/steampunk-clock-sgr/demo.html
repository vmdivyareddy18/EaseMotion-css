Steampunk Gear Train & Mechanical Clock

An animation-first, interactive, pure CSS mechanical clockwork simulation featuring cogs meshing at mathematically exact gear ratios.
How it works: The clockwork uses four interlocking gear cogs with proportional tooth counts (24T, 12T, and two 8T cogs). By matching their rotation durations mathematically to their sizes (e.g. 12s, 6s, and 4s) and alternating clockwise/counter-clockwise directions, the cogs mesh seamlessly. The clock hands tick in sync with the central gear. Use the dashboard below to switch Aesthetics (Classic Brass vs Cyber Neon), Winding Speed (slow Cruise vs fast Wind-up), or toggle Reverse Mode—all natively in pure CSS.
