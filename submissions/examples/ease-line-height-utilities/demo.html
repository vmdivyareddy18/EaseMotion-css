Line Height Utilities (Leading)

Control the vertical spacing between lines of text using proportional line-height utility classes.

1. Tight (.ease-leading-tight - 1.25)

Best for large headings where standard line-height creates too much awkward white space between wrapped lines.

This is a massive
display heading
with tight spacing.

2. Base / Normal (.ease-leading-normal - 1.5)

The ideal, accessible line-height for standard paragraph reading text.

Typography is the art and technique of arranging type to make written language legible, readable, and appealing when displayed. The arrangement of type involves selecting typefaces, point sizes, line lengths, line-spacing (leading), and letter-spacing (tracking).

3. Loose (.ease-leading-loose - 2)

Extra airy text, sometimes used in poetry, legal documents, or highly stylized editorial layouts.

A very airy paragraph
that breathes heavily
between every single line
creating a unique rhythm.
