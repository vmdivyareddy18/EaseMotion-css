Reduced Motion Access Center

Targeted accessibility audit demonstrating the transition from physics-heavy motion classes to accessible, user-friendly overrides.

Standard Mode

Clicking the toggle below applies the .emulate-reduced-motion container class to showcase the exact behavior users experience when their system preference is set to reduce motion.
