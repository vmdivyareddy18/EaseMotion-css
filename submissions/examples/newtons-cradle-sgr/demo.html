Newton's Cradle Physics Sandbox

An animation-first, interactive, pure CSS physics simulation utilizing synchronized momentum transfer keyframes.
How it works: The sandbox simulates elastic collision physics using synchronized keyframe animations. Upon impact, momentum transfers instantly through the stationary center balls to swing the opposite side. You can use the controls in the dashboard to switch the Swing Mode (1 Ball, 2 Balls, or Double-Ended), change Simulation Speed (Slow-Mo vs Hyper), or toggle the **Neon Glow** overlay—all natively in pure CSS.
