Vibrant Frosted Glass

Using backdrop-filter: saturate() blur() to create premium iOS-style glassmorphism that enhances the colors behind it.

🧊

Standard Glass

Just `blur(16px)`. The colors behind are diffused, but they look muddy and muted through the glass.

✨

Vibrant Glass

Adding `saturate(180%)`. Notice how the background colors pop and look much more premium through the glass.
