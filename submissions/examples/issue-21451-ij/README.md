## Shooting Star Animation

A luminous shooting star blazes across the cosmos with a trailing glow, set against a deep space background with twinkling stars. A second, smaller meteor follows at a delay.

**Features:**
- Meteor with bright head and graduated tail via pseudo-elements
- Two meteors at different trajectories and delays
- Twinkling background stars for depth
- Respects `prefers-reduced-motion`
