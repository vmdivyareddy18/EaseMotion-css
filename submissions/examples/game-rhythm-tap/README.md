# 🎵 Rhythm Tap

A rhythm game where you tap along to a beat pattern. Follow the visual cues, tap in time with the music, and improve your accuracy as the tempo increases!

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Click **"Start Game"** or press **Space** to begin
2. **Tap** the circle or press **Space** in time with the beat
3. **Watch the visual cues** – the beat track shows upcoming notes
4. **Hit the beats** accurately to score points
5. **Level up** as you complete patterns
6. **Tempo increases** with each level!

## ⌨️ Controls

| Action | Input |
|--------|-------|
| Tap / Start | Click circle or press `Space` |
| Reset Game | Press `R` |
| Change Tempo | Press `T` or click Tempo button |

## ✨ Features

- 🎵 **Rhythm-Based Gameplay** – Tap along to the beat
- 🎯 **Accuracy Tracking** – See your hit percentage
- 📈 **Progressive Difficulty** – Level up with faster tempos
- 🎨 **Visual Feedback** – Hit/miss animations and color cues
- 🎤 **Audio Feedback** – Sound effects for hits and misses
- 📊 **Score System** – Points for accurate taps
- 🏆 **Best Score** – Saved in browser local storage
- 📱 **Responsive Design** – Works on desktop and mobile
- 🖱️ **Touch Support** – Tap on mobile devices

## 🎯 Scoring

| Action | Points |
|--------|--------|
| Accurate Hit | +10 + (Accuracy Bonus) |
| Missed Beat | -5 |
| Tap on Empty Beat | -3 |
| Level Bonus | +10 per 10% accuracy |

## 📊 Difficulty Progression

| Level | Tempo (BPM) | Pattern Complexity |
|-------|-------------|-------------------|
| 1 | 80 | Simple, mostly downbeats |
| 2 | 100 | Some off-beats |
| 3 | 120 | Syncopation introduced |
| 4 | 140 | Complex patterns |
| 5 | 160 | Advanced syncopation |
| 6+ | 180 | Maximum challenge |
