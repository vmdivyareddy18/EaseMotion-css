Where should I post?

We've structured discussions into specific categories to help you find answers and share knowledge effectively.

Q&A

Ask questions about how to use EaseMotion classes, troubleshoot bugs in your implementation, or seek advice.

Ideas

Propose new animation utility classes, easing curves, or structural improvements to the framework.

Show & Tell

Created something awesome using EaseMotion? Show off your projects, landing pages, and component designs here!

General

Anything else related to CSS animations, web performance, or community announcements.

Community Guidelines

  • Search before asking. Someone may have already answered your question! Use the search bar before starting a new thread.
  • Provide context (and code!). If asking for help, include a CodePen or minimal reproducible HTML/CSS snippet.
  • Mark answers as correct. If someone solves your issue in the Q&A category, mark their reply as the answer to help future visitors.
  • Be excellent to each other. Keep feedback constructive, welcoming, and inclusive. We are all here to learn and build cool things.
