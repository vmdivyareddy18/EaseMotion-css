Object Position Utilities

CSS object-position utilities to adjust the alignment of replaced media elements (like images or video) inside their layout boxes when resized.

Vertical Positions (Wide 250x180 Containers)

In these wide containers, a square (1:1) image overflows vertically. The vertical position shifts the image up or down, while the horizontal alignment is centered.

Top Position .op-top

Aligns the top edge of the media with the top edge of the container box. The bottom area is cropped out, making the yellow TOP indicator visible.

Center Position .op-center

Centers the media horizontally and vertically. Cuts off equal amounts of the top and bottom, showing the purple CENTER circle clearly.

Bottom Position .op-bottom

Aligns the bottom edge of the media with the bottom edge of the container box. The top area is cropped, showing the pink BOTTOM indicator.

Horizontal Positions (Tall 180x250 Containers)

In these tall containers, the square image overflows horizontally. Shifting the position horizontally reveals left or right parts of the image.

Left Position .op-left

Aligns the left edge of the media with the left of the container. Crops the right side, showing the orange LEFT label.

Center Position .op-center

Centers the image horizontally, clipping equal parts on the left and right, leaving the purple CENTER circle in the middle.

Right Position .op-right

Aligns the right edge of the media with the right of the container. Crops the left side, showing the green RIGHT label.

Corner / Diagonal Positions (Wide 250x180 Containers)

These classes align the media content to the corners. In these wide containers, since vertical space is what overflows, the vertical component (top vs bottom) dictates the view.

Top Left .op-top-left

Aligns the content to the top-left. Demonstrates vertical alignment to the top showing the cyan TOP-LEFT indicator.

Top Right .op-top-right

Aligns the content to the top-right. Highlights the top vertical position showing the blue T-RIGHT (Top Right) label.

Bottom Left .op-bottom-left

Aligns the content to the bottom-left. Focuses on the bottom vertical position showing the red B-LEFT (Bottom Left) indicator.

Bottom Right .op-bottom-right

Aligns the content to the bottom-right. Focuses on the bottom vertical position showing the green-yellow B-RIGHT (Bottom Right) label.
