Radial masking mechanics in pure CSS

  • clip-path: circle(0% at 50% 50%); locks the hidden layer to a zero-radius circle resting precisely at the card center coordinates.
  • clip-path: circle(141.4% at 50% 50%); on hover expands the circle diagonal radius so it covers 100% of the rectangular card boundaries.
  • transition-delay: [ms]; controls inner staggers. By setting delayed transitions for header, metrics, and buttons, the content slides upward sequentially inside the expanding circular boundary.
