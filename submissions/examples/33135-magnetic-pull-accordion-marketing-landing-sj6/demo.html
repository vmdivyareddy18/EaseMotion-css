How does the 14-day free trial work?

Every plan starts with all features unlocked for 14 days — no credit card required. When the trial ends, your workspace simply drops to the free tier; nothing is deleted and nothing is charged.

Can I change plans later?

Anytime. Upgrades apply immediately and we prorate the difference; downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle so you never lose time you already paid for.

What happens if I cancel?

Your plan stays active until the end of the period you paid for, then the workspace moves to the free tier. You can export everything at any time — your data is yours.

How are team seats billed?

Per active member, per month. Invite as many viewers as you like for free — only editors count as seats, and removing a seat credits the unused time back to your account.
