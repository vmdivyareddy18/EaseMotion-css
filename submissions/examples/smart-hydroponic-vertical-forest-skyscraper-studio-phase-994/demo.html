Frequently Asked Questions

How does it grow without soil?
We use advanced aeroponic and hydroponic systems. Roots are suspended in the air or inert mediums and directly supplied with highly oxygenated, nutrient-rich water. This eliminates soil-borne pests and maximizes growth speed.
What happens during a power outage?
The skyscraper is equipped with integrated solar glass panels and gravity-fed emergency water reserves that can sustain the entire ecosystem for up to 72 hours without external grid power.
Who maintains the plants?
90% of maintenance is handled by our AI-driven robotic systems, from seed planting to pruning and harvesting. Human botanists conduct weekly oversight inspections.
