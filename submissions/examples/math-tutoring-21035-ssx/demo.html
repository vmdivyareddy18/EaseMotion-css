Our Teaching Method

The 4-step Northstar method

A clear, repeatable process — built from 13 years of figuring out what actually moves grades.

01

Diagnose

A free 45-minute assessment identifies exactly which concepts your student has mastered, half-learned, or never seen.

02

Build a Plan

We write a custom 12-week roadmap with weekly goals, practice sets, and a clear definition of "done".

03

Teach & Practice

Weekly 1-on-1 sessions plus targeted practice between meetings. We teach why, not just how.

04

Measure

Monthly parent report with quiz scores, mastery checks, and a clear comparison to the starting baseline.
