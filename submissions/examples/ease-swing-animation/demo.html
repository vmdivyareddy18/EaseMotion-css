ease-swing

Pendulum swing from transform-origin: top center. Starts at rotate(-15deg), swings to rotate(15deg), then diminishes to rest at 0. Triggered by adding the class.

Default trigger

Swing

Variants

Fast
.ease-swing-fast (0.5s)
Slow
.ease-swing-slow (2s)
Wide
.ease-swing-wide (±25deg)
