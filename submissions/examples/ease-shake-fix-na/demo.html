The Fix Explained

By containing the element within an overflow-x: hidden wrapper block, we isolate the aggressive translateX shifts from the global viewport.

ease-shake-wrapper

📄 Safe Implementation Structure

Wrap your shaking elements like this to stay mobile-friendly:

  <div class="ease-shake">
    Safe content on mobile screens
  </div>
