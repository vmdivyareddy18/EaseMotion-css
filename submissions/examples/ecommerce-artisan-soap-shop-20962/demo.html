THE SLOW WAY

Cured for 6 weeks.

Commercial soaps strip natural glycerin from their bars, leaving skin tight and dry. We use the traditional cold-process method, which retains 100% of the moisturizing glycerin.

Every batch is poured by hand, cut by wire, and cured on wooden racks for six full weeks to ensure a hard, long-lasting bar with a luxurious lather.
