# Theremin

A pure CSS and HTML theremin, the instrument you play without touching, two hands weaving in the electromagnetic fields around its antennas.

## What it shows

- Two hands built from a palm and jointed fingers, one gliding along the vertical pitch antenna, the other bobbing over the looped volume antenna
- Pulsing dashed field rings around each antenna and radio waves emitting from the tips
- The wooden cabinet, control panel and slotted knobs from gradients and pseudo elements
- Fingers subtly wiggling as the hands shape the note
- No images, no JavaScript, no external dependencies

## Usage

Open `demo.html` in any modern browser.

## Accessibility

All motion stops under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.
