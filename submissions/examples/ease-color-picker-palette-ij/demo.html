ease-color-picker-palette-ij

Color picker palette that reveals with a staggered swatch expansion animation.

Click to open palette

Click the swatch trigger to reveal the palette — each swatch expands in with a staggered delay. Click a swatch to select it.

Hover to expand palette strip

Hover or focus the swatch anchor — the strip expands and swatches pop in one after another.

Customized via CSS variables

Larger swatches, wider gap, faster expansion — all via --ecpp-swatch-size, --ecpp-gap, --ecpp-speed.
