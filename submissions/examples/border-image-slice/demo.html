How Slicing Works

The source image is divided into nine regions by four slice lines (top, right, bottom, left). Corner regions are placed at the element's corners. Edge regions stretch or repeat along the sides. The center is discarded unless fill is specified.

All examples use a gradient source image via border-image-source.
