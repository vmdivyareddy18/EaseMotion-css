# Music Theory Online Course Template
A melodic, precise, and educational template for music theory courses, conservatory prep, and composition masterclasses.

- **Design**: Uses a "Harmonic-Precision" palette with rhythm-blue and harmony-purple gradients, staff-inspired neutral backgrounds, and clean, modular lesson cards to signify academic depth and artistic creativity.
- **Use Case**: Music theory learning portals, composition masterclasses, and music conservatory preparation.
