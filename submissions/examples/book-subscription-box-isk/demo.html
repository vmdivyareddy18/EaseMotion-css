Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions? We've got answers. Feel free to contact our support desk.

How are the books curated?
Our team collaborates with major publishers to review advance copies months in advance. We select the highest-rated novels based on storytelling depth, diversity of author voices, and critical acclaim.
Can I change my preferred genre?
Yes! You can update your reading profile settings before the 1st of the month to choose a different genre theme for the upcoming box.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, we ship to over 40 countries worldwide. International shipping rates are calculated at checkout based on location.
