Color Adjust Utilities

CSS color-adjust and print-color-adjust utilities to regulate how background colors, gradients, and text indicators render on printed media and high-contrast views.

What is print-color-adjust?
Most browsers strip background colors and image gradients when printing to save ink/toner. Using print-color-adjust: exact overrides this, forcing the printer to preserve brand identity, logos, and colored highlight borders.
