Animated Modal / Overlay Component

Four animation variants built on the existing .ease-modal-overlay + .ease-modal system. Click any button to open a modal. Press Esc or click the backdrop/close button to dismiss.

Scale-up Modal

The default .ease-modal entry animation — scales up from 90% with a bounce easing. Defined in components/modals.css.

This is the standard modal pattern. It fades in with a satisfying elastic bounce on open.

Slide from Top

The modal slides down from above with a bounce easing — great for notifications, alerts, or confirmation dialogs that feel like they drop in from the nav bar.

Bottom Sheet

A mobile-friendly bottom sheet modal — slides up from the bottom of the viewport. Rounded top corners, full width, capped at 80vh.

Ideal for mobile action sheets, filter panels, and share menus.

3D Flip Modal

A 3D perspective flip entry — the modal rotates in on the X axis from above using rotateX(-25deg) with perspective: 1200px.

Eye-catching for onboarding flows, feature announcements, or achievement dialogs.

Side Drawer

A right-side drawer panel — translates in from translateX(100%). Full viewport height, rounded left corners.

Ideal for settings panels, navigation menus, shopping carts, and filter sidebars.

Dark Theme Modal

Any variant works with dark theming — applies automatically via prefers-color-scheme: dark or the [data-theme="dark"] attribute.
