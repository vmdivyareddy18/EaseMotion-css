Fade Slide Carousel

A fade slide carousel with crossfade transitions and dot navigation.

Demo

Click dots to navigate. Auto‑play is on by default.

Slide 1 — Indigo
Slide 2 — Pink
Slide 3 — Green
Slide 4 — Amber

Usage

<div class="carousel" style="--carousel-width: 600px; --carousel-height: 340px; --fade-duration: 0.8s; --dot-color: #6366f1;">
  <div class="carousel-track">
    <div class="carousel-slide active">Slide 1</div>
    <div class="carousel-slide">Slide 2</div>
    <div class="carousel-slide">Slide 3</div>
  </div>
  <nav class="carousel-dots"></nav>
</div>
