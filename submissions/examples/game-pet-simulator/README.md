# 🐾 Pet Simulator

A virtual pet simulation game where you care for a digital companion. Feed, play, clean, and sleep with your pet to keep it happy and healthy. Watch it grow and evolve as you level up!

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Feed** 🍖 – Keeps your pet from getting hungry
2. **Play** 🎾 – Increases happiness and bonds with your pet
3. **Clean** 🧹 – Keeps your pet fresh and happy
4. **Sleep** 😴 – Restores energy (toggle on/off)
5. **Click** your pet – Gives it love and attention

## ⌨️ Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `F` | Feed |
| `P` | Play |
| `C` | Clean |
| `S` | Sleep |
| `R` | Reset |
| Click Pet | Give Love |

## ✨ Features

- 🐾 **4 Stats** – Happiness, Hunger, Energy, Level
- 🎯 **Leveling System** – Gain XP from actions, level up
- 🎨 **Pet Evolution** – Change emoji at certain levels
- 💤 **Sleep System** – Restores energy over time
- 🎲 **Random Events** – Unexpected bonuses
- 📊 **Real-time Progression** – Stats decay over time
- ✏️ **Rename Pet** – Customize your companion
- 🎮 **Interactive** – Click pet for love boost
- 📱 **Responsive Design** – Works on all devices

## 📊 Stats

| Stat | Description |
|------|-------------|
| ❤️ Happiness | Affected by play, clean, and love |
| 🍖 Hunger | Decreases over time; feed to restore |
| ⚡ Energy | Decreases with activity; sleep to restore |
| 🎯 Level | Increases with XP from actions |

## 🎯 Evolution

Your pet evolves every 3 levels:
- Level 1-2: 🐶 Dog
- Level 3-5: 🐱 Cat
- Level 6-8: 🐰 Rabbit
- Level 9-11: 🐹 Hamster
- Level 12-14: 🐦 Bird
- Level 15+: 🐠 Fish, 🦊 Fox, 🐼 Panda
