# Hourglass Sand Animation

A mesmerizing, frame-by-frame sand particle animation of an hourglass built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Watch sand particles fall from the top chamber through the neck into the bottom chamber in real-time — no dependencies required.

---

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Realistic particle physics** – Gravity, friction, and collision
- ✅ **Frame-by-frame animation** – Smooth 60fps rendering
- ✅ **Interactive controls** – Play/Pause, Reset, Speed control
- ✅ **Keyboard shortcuts** – Space (pause), R (reset), S (speed)
- ✅ **Responsive** – Adapts to any screen size
- ✅ **Particle count display** – Shows sand in top and bottom
- ✅ **Light/Dark mode** – Auto-adjusts to system preference
- ✅ **Zero dependencies** – No libraries or frameworks
- ✅ **Accessible** – Keyboard controls and ARIA support
- ✅ **Customizable** – Easy to tweak colors and physics
