Scroll-Animated Timeline (Pure CSS, No JavaScript)

Each timeline entry slides in and fades up as it scrolls into view, using animation-timeline: view() — zero JavaScript, no IntersectionObserver. Scroll down slowly to see each item reveal.

💡 Browser support: Chrome/Edge 115+ animate the reveal on scroll. Firefox/Safari fall back to a static, always-visible timeline — no broken layout, just no scroll-tied motion.
