About

I'm Alex Chen, a frontend engineer and technical writer based in San Francisco. I've spent the last decade building tools for developers — from bundlers to design systems to documentation platforms.

Currently, I lead Developer Experience at Vercel, where I work on making the web faster and more accessible for everyone. Before that, I was at Stripe and Mozilla.

When I'm not coding, I'm usually hiking the Pacific Coast Trail or brewing pour-over coffee. I believe the best software is built by teams that care about both craft and kindness.
