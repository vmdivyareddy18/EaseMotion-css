How to Use

Add the markup structure below to your page, link the stylesheet `style.css`, and initialize the mousemove listeners. Add the class `.ease-tilt-depth` along with `.ease-depth-low`, `.ease-depth-medium`, or `.ease-depth-high` to any inner elements you want to pop out in 3D parallax.

HTML Markup Structure

<div class="ease-tilt-container">
  <div class="ease-tilt-card">
    <!-- Reflection overlay -->
    <div class="ease-tilt-glare"></div>
    
    <!-- Card Inner Contents -->
    <div class="ease-tilt-content">
      <!-- High Parallax Depth element -->
      <div class="ease-tilt-depth ease-depth-high">
        <!-- Content Here (e.g. Floating Logo) -->
      </div>
      
      <!-- Medium Parallax Depth Title -->
      <h3 class="ease-tilt-depth ease-depth-medium">Card Title</h3>
      
      <!-- Low Parallax Depth Description -->
      <p class="ease-tilt-depth ease-depth-low">Card Description...</p>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
