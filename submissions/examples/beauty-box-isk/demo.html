Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our cosmetics subscriptions and deliveries.

Can I customize the products in my box?
Yes! After signing up, you will fill out a Beauty Profile where you can specify your skin type (oily, dry, combination), skin tone, and color preferences. Our curators customize your monthly selections based on these settings.
When does the box ship each month?
We package and ship all boxes on the 5th of every month. Standard shipping takes 3-5 business days within the continental USA. You will receive tracking details via email as soon as the label is scanned.
What is the cancellation policy?
You can cancel or pause your monthly subscription anytime before the 1st of the month. No termination fees, no hidden questions. Just access your settings and click the pause subscription trigger.
