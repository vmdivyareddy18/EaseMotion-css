# Water Wheel

A pure CSS and HTML overshot mill water wheel turning under a falling millrace, beside its timber mill house.

## What it shows

- Eight paddles placed around the hub with the rotate then translateY radial pattern, each parking its own angle in a custom property
- Four spokes on the same radial trick, all carried round by one rotation on the wheel group
- A falling water chute and a flowing river from vertical and horizontal repeating linear gradients that scroll via background-position
- Splash droplets bursting where the wheel meets the water
- No images, no JavaScript, no external dependencies

## Usage

Open `demo.html` in any modern browser.

## Accessibility

All motion stops under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.
