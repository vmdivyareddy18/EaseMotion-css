Designing readable motion systems

Section markers help readers understand where they are before they even reach the heading. A small labeled pill can communicate hierarchy, pace, and structure in a way that feels lightweight.

Overview

Keep orientation simple and visible

These markers work well in docs pages, storytelling layouts, and long-form product writing where content needs gentle visual anchors.

Key Insight

Hierarchy should support, not dominate

When the marker stays compact and motion stays subtle, the heading remains the main focus while the label adds context.
