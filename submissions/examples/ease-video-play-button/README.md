# Video Play Button – Pulsing play button over thumbnail

> A production-ready video play button component featuring a pulsing play button overlay on thumbnails with play/pause functionality and visual feedback. Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—no dependencies required.

---

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Pulsing play button** – Animated pulse and ring effect
- ✅ **Thumbnail overlay** – Play button centered on thumbnail
- ✅ **Play/Pause/Stop** – Full video state control
- ✅ **Toggle pulse** – Enable/disable pulse animation
- ✅ **Keyboard shortcuts** – Space (play/pause), Escape (stop)
- ✅ **Multiple sizes** – Small, Medium, Large, Dark variants
- ✅ **Responsive** – Adapts to any screen size
- ✅ **Light/Dark mode** – Auto-adjusts to system preference
- ✅ **Zero dependencies** – No libraries or frameworks
