# Gravity Button – Button that falls and bounces when clicked

> A production-ready gravity button component that simulates realistic physics — click to launch the button upward, watch it fall and bounce with adjustable gravity, bounce, and friction. Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—no dependencies required.

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Realistic physics** – Gravity, bounce, and friction simulation
- ✅ **Click to launch** – Button flies up and falls with physics
- ✅ **Adjustable parameters** – Gravity, bounce, friction sliders
- ✅ **Bounce counter** – Tracks number of bounces
- ✅ **Physics toggle** – Enable/disable physics
- ✅ **Randomize** – Random physics settings
- ✅ **Keyboard shortcuts** – Space (launch), R (reset), P (toggle)
- ✅ **4 variants** – Small, Large, Bouncy, Heavy
- ✅ **Responsive** – Adapts to any screen size
- ✅ **Light/Dark mode** – Auto-adjusts to system preference
- ✅ **Zero dependencies** – No libraries or frameworks
