# Advanced 3D Interactive Glassmorphism Card

A frosted glass card component demonstrating 3D tilt matrices and glow reflect overlays.

---

## What is Included

1. **`demo.html`**: A showcase dashboard hosting 3D perspective grids, reflective overlays, text blocks, and margins.
2. **`style.css`**: Backdrop blur levels, translateZ elevations, glass borders, and outline colors.

---

## Verification Steps

1. Open `demo.html` in a web browser.
2. Move your cursor across the frosted glass card.
3. Verify that the card tilts in 3D perspective, and the light reflect overlay moves smoothly tracking the cursor position.
