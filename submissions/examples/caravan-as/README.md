# Caravan

A pure CSS and HTML camel caravan plodding across the dunes as a huge sun sinks into the desert horizon.

## What it shows

- Camels in silhouette, their legs stepping in a natural alternating gait driven by two mirrored keyframes
- Three camels at different scales, each parking its scale in a custom property so the plod keyframe can bob them without wiping it, and spaced in depth
- Twin humps, a curved neck and a lifted head assembled from rounded boxes
- A vast setting sun and layered dunes for the sunset horizon
- No images, no JavaScript, no external dependencies

## Usage

Open `demo.html` in any modern browser.

## Accessibility

All motion stops under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.
