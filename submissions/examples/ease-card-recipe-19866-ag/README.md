# CSS Cooking Recipe Card Placeholder

This submission details the `.ease-card-recipe` component, optimized for cooking lists, recipe aggregators, and food blogging pages.

---

## Acceptance Criteria Handled

- **Food Image Hover Zoom:** The recipe thumbnail expands smoothly by `8%` on hover within its container bounds.
- **Ingredients Badge:** Absolute overlay at the top-right of the image stating ingredient counts.
- **Recipe Details:** Includes recipe Title, excerpt descriptions, and cooking/prep meta details.
- **Difficulty Flames:** A five-flame indicator displaying recipe difficulty using active and inactive emoji filters.

---

## Verification Steps

1. Open `demo.html` in a web browser.
2. Hover over the recipe card. Verify:
   - The card lifts vertically by `4px` with a cyan perimeter border glow.
   - The image scales up smoothly.
3. Verify that details (Prep: 15 mins, Cook: 20 mins) align correctly.
4. Verify the 3-flame difficulty indicator renders with appropriate coloring.
