.ease-scroll-reveal

Fade-in-up tied to scroll

This card animates based on its position in the viewport — not on time. Scroll up and down to scrub the animation directly.

.ease-scroll-zoom

Zoom in as it enters view

Scales from 0.8 to 1.0 as the element enters the viewport, driven entirely by scroll position via animation-timeline: view().

.ease-scroll-blur

Unblur on scroll

Starts blurred and sharpens as you scroll it into view. No JavaScript blur calculation needed.

.ease-view-slide-in-left

Slides in from the left

Uses animation-range: entry 0% entry 70% so the slide completes before the element is fully in view.

.ease-view-slide-in-right

Slides in from the right

Mirror of slide-in-left — useful for alternating left/right reveal patterns down a page.

.ease-view-reveal

Generic view-timeline reveal

The base reveal utility — fade + slide-up, driven by animation-timeline: view() across the full entry range.

.ease-scroll-parallax

This text drifts upward

Driven by animation-timeline: view() with animation-range: cover 0% cover 100% — moves continuously across the full time the element is in view.
