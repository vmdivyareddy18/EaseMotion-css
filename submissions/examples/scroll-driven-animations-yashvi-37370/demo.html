Slide Reveals

🚀

Slide Up Entrance

Standard viewport entry transition. Slides elements upwards into position as you scroll down. Excellent for listing key features, posts, or columns.

⚙️

Slide Up (Delayed Offset)

Uses custom CSS variables to modify the entrance viewport range offset dynamically (`--scroll-range: entry 25% cover 50%`).

Zoom & Elastic Scaling

🔍

Zoom In Reveal

Scales element from 0.7 up to 1 as it traverses the scroll boundaries. Provides a dramatic, visual impact for showcases or imagery layouts.

💎

Zoom In (Custom Curve)

Integrates smoothly with design tokens to scale boundaries. Perfectly responsive and adjusts dynamically on resizing.

Lateral Entrances

⬅️

Slide In From Left

Slides horizontally from the left side of the screen. Adds a staggered flow when paired with items entering from the right.

➡️

Slide In From Right

Slides horizontally from the right side of the screen. Combines with the left entrance for complex visual layout entrances.
