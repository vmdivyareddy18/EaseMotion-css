FAQ

Questions? We've got answers.

How long does it take to get fluent?
It depends on the language and your starting point, but most learners reach conversational fluency (B1/B2) in 6–12 months with 20–30 minutes of daily practice. Lingora's adaptive system means every minute counts — no time wasted on things you already know.
Is the free plan really free? No credit card required?
Yes, genuinely free. No credit card, no trial period, no hidden charges. The free plan gives you 5 lessons per day forever. We make money from Premium subscribers who want more.
Which languages are available?
We currently offer 40+ languages including Spanish, French, German, Japanese, Korean, Mandarin, Arabic, Hindi, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Turkish, Dutch, Swedish, Polish, and more. New languages are added every quarter.
Can I use Lingora offline?
Offline mode is available on Premium and Teams plans. You can download your current learning unit and practice without an internet connection. Your progress syncs automatically when you're back online.
Are the CEFR certificates officially recognised?
Our certificates are accepted by 800+ universities and many employers as a recognised indicator of proficiency. They're not a replacement for official exams like DELF or JLPT, but they're a credible first step and great for portfolios and CVs.
Can I cancel my Premium subscription anytime?
Yes. Cancel anytime from your account settings. If you're on the annual plan, you'll keep Premium access until the end of your billing period. No cancellation fees, no awkward phone calls.
