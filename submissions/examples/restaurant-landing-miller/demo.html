Our Story

Built around an open flame

Ember & Vine started as a single wood-fired oven in chef Lena Marchetti's backyard, cooking for neighbors on Sunday afternoons. Eleven years later the fire is bigger, but the idea hasn't changed — simple ingredients, real heat, and a table that's always got room for one more.

Every dish on the menu passes over the hearth at some point, whether it's the char on the octopus or the smoke folded into the chocolate tart. Nothing's frozen. Nothing's rushed.

— Chef Lena Marchetti, Founder
