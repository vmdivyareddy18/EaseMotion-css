# Contact Business Card

## What does this do?
A digital business card with a 3D flip animation, showing contact details (email, phone, social links) on the back face with copy-to-clipboard functionality.

## How is it used?
Open demo.html in a browser. Click the card to flip it and reveal contact information. Click the copy icon next to the email to copy it to the clipboard.

## Why is this useful?
Demonstrates a polished 3D card flip UI pattern with practical clipboard integration and social link icons.

## Tech Stack
- HTML
- CSS (no frameworks)
- Vanilla JavaScript

## Preview
Open demo.html directly in your browser.
