What is Neo-Brutalism?

Neo-brutalism is a web design trend characterized by stark contrasts, solid, unblurred drop shadows, and thick borders. It rejects the soft gradients and blurs of flat design or glassmorphism in favor of a raw, mechanical look.

Does this require JavaScript?

Is it accessible?

Yes. Because it uses semantic HTML tags, screen readers and keyboard navigation natively understand how to interact with and read the accordion items.
