The Art of Pouring

Every Lumina candle is hand-poured in small batches in our studio. We use 100% natural soy wax derived from American-grown soybeans for an eco-friendly, clean burn.

Our cotton wicks are lead-free, and our fragrances are blended using premium essential oils and phthalate-free fragrance oils. No additives, no dyes—just pure, comforting light.
