# Legal Hold Case Tracker

A high-fidelity **Legal Hold Case Tracker** built entirely with HTML and CSS.

## Overview

This example demonstrates an enterprise legal governance dashboard for managing litigation hold notices, preserving evidence, tracking custodian acknowledgements, and monitoring legal hold compliance throughout the lifecycle of corporate investigations and regulatory matters.

The interface combines executive governance KPIs, an advanced legal hold register, custodian workflows, compliance analytics, legal timelines, and evidence preservation summaries into a responsive enterprise dashboard suitable for legal operations teams.

---

## Features

- Enterprise legal governance dashboard
- Advanced legal hold register
- Sticky case tracking table
- Executive governance KPI cards
- Drafted notice tracking
- Issued hold monitoring
- Custodian acknowledgement management
- Overdue notice alerts
- Released hold lifecycle tracking
- Interactive legal hold checklist
- Case lifecycle timeline
- Animated governance compliance score
- Hold progress analytics
- Evidence preservation overview
- Responsive enterprise layout
- Pure HTML & CSS (No JavaScript)
