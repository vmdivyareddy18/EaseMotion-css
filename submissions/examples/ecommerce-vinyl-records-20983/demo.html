The Grading Guide

We grade strictly to Goldmine standards using high-intensity light and play-testing.

M / NM

Mint / Near Mint

Perfect record. No scuffs, scratches, or wear. Sleeve is crisp with no ring wear.

VG+

Very Good Plus

Shows slight signs of wear, light scuffs or very light scratches that don't affect listening.

VG

Very Good

More evident wear. Surface noise may be evident on soft passages or during intro/fade, but won't overpower the music.

G+

Good Plus

Significant wear. Will play through without skipping, but has significant surface noise, scratches, and visible groove wear.
