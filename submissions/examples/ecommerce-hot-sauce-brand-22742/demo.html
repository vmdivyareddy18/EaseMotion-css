Inferno started in a cramped apartment kitchen with a blender and a dream to make a sauce that actually hurts. We were tired of "hot" sauces that were mostly vinegar and empty promises.

Today, we source the most aggressive peppers directly from independent farmers, fermenting them for 90 days to develop complex flavors before they destroy your taste buds.
