# Email Marketing SaaS Landing Page

## What does this do?
It creates a full responsive landing page for an email marketing SaaS with campaign preview, feature cards, and analytics highlights.

## How is it used?
```html
<section class="hero">
  <h1>Create newsletters, automations, and launches from one calm workspace.</h1>
  <a class="button" href="#campaigns">Build a campaign</a>
</section>
```

## Why is it useful?
It demonstrates a complete marketing SaaS layout with subtle campaign motion and a self-contained CSS-only implementation.
