# Business Valuator Template
A rigorous, data-centric, and professional template for business valuation firms, corporate finance advisors, and M&A analysts.

- **Design**: Uses a "Precision-Analytic" palette with valuation-navy primary blues, market-gold accents, and clean, report-style layouts to emphasize accuracy, independence, and technical expertise.
- **Use Case**: Business valuation firms, M&A advisory practices, and corporate financial reporting consultants.
