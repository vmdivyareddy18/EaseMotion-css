OS-Level Dark Mode Refactor

This architectural proposal actively and aggressively rips out toxic, hardcoded hex colors and completely replaces them with semantic, dual-state CSS variables natively tied to the highly optimized prefers-color-scheme media query.

To see the magic: Toggle your computer or phone's operating system into Dark Mode right now. Watch the Modern Card perfectly adapt instantly, while the Legacy Card remains permanently broken and blindingly white.

❌ Legacy Hardcoded Hex

Uses background: #ffffff directly inside the component CSS. It completely ignores your operating system preferences and will violently blind you at night.

I am blindingly bright!

I physically cannot adapt to Dark Mode without massive, messy CSS overrides.

✅ Modern Semantic Variables

Uses background: var(--ease-surface). The variable value mathematically swaps perfectly and instantly based entirely on your native OS dark mode setting.

I adapt perfectly to your OS!

I am highly responsive to OS-level prefers-color-scheme queries automatically.
