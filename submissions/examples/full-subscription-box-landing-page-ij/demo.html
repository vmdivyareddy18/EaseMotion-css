Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change or cancel my plan anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your subscription at any time. No hidden fees, no penalties.

When will I receive my first box?

Your first box ships within 3–5 business days after subscribing. Subsequent boxes ship on the 5th of each month.

Do you ship internationally?

We ship to over 40 countries. Shipping fees vary by location. Free shipping is available in the US, Canada, and UK.

What if I don't like something in my box?

We stand by our curation. If you're not satisfied with any item, we'll refund it or send a replacement — no questions asked.
