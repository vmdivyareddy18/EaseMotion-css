Canine Puppy Guidelines

Initial developmental stages require intensive immunization and training support.

  • Core vaccinations (DHPP, Rabies) starting at 6-8 weeks.
  • De-worming treatment cycles every 3-4 weeks.
  • Puppy training, crate socialization, and chew monitoring.
  • High-protein development diet twice daily.
