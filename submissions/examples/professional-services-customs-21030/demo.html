The Clearance Process

How we move your goods from origin to destination without friction.

1

Document Receipt

Commercial invoices, packing lists, and bill of lading are submitted to our digital portal for immediate review.

2

Classification & Filing

Our licensed brokers classify goods via HTSUS and transmit the entry summary electronically to Customs.

3

Duty Payment

Duties, taxes, and fees are calculated and paid automatically via our ACH account to expedite release.

4

Release & Delivery

Customs issues the release authorization. Your carrier is notified instantly to pick up the freight.
