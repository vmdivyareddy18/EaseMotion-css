Collapsible Section

Smooth height animation

A collapsible section is a UI component that hides and reveals content with a smooth height animation. It's perfect for:

  • ✅ FAQ sections
  • ✅ Accordion menus
  • ✅ Progressive disclosure
  • ✅ Organizing large content

The smooth height transition creates a polished, professional feel.

This component is highly customizable:

  • 🎨 Colors: Match your brand
  • 📏 Size: Adjustable padding and fonts
  • ⏱️ Animation: Control speed and easing
  • 🔄 Behavior: Accordion or independent
