Announce Bar Pattern Library

5 reusable announcement bar patterns

1. Sticky Promo Bar

Fixed top announcement with slide-down entrance

🎉 Summer Sale! Get 30% off all components Shop Now → 
<div class="ease-announce-bar promo-bar ease-slide-down">
  <div class="announce-content">
    <span>🎉 Summer Sale! Get 30% off</span>
    <a href="#">Shop Now →</a>
  </div>
</div>

2. Dismissible Slide-down

Alert bar with close button and slide-down entrance

⚠️ New feature available! Check out the new dashboard 
<div class="ease-announce-bar alert-bar ease-slide-down" id="alertBar">
  <div class="announce-content">
    <span>⚠️ New feature available!</span>
  </div>
  <button class="announce-close" onclick="dismissBar('alertBar')">×</button>
</div>

3. Rotating Messages Bar

Multiple announcements cycling through

📢
Free shipping on orders over $50 New component: Neon Pulse Button Join our Discord community! 
<div class="ease-announce-bar rotate-bar">
  <div class="announce-content">
    <span>📢</span>
    <div class="rotate-messages">
      <span class="rotate-msg active">Free shipping on orders over $50</span>
      <span class="rotate-msg">New component available!</span>
      <span class="rotate-msg">Join our community!</span>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

4. Sale Countdown Strip

Countdown timer with urgency messaging

Sale ends in:
23 : 59 : 59
Don't miss out! 
<div class="ease-announce-bar countdown-bar ease-fade-in">
  <div class="announce-content">
    <span>Sale ends in:</span>
    <div class="countdown-timer">
      <span>23</span>:<span>59</span>:<span>59</span>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>

5. Cookie Consent Bar

Info bar with accept and settings buttons

🍪 We use cookies to improve your experience 
<div class="ease-announce-bar cookie-bar ease-slide-up" id="cookieBar">
  <div class="announce-content">
    <span>🍪 We use cookies to improve your experience</span>
  </div>
  <div class="cookie-actions">
    <button>Settings</button>
    <button onclick="dismissBar('cookieBar')">Accept</button>
  </div>
</div>
