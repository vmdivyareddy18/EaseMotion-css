# Food Safety Inspection Scorecard

A high-fidelity **Food Safety Inspection Scorecard** built entirely with HTML and CSS.

## Overview

This example demonstrates an enterprise food safety compliance dashboard for managing inspections across restaurants, warehouses, processing plants, distribution centers, and retail facilities. It combines executive food safety KPIs, inspection scorecards, hygiene assessments, temperature monitoring, corrective action tracking, and compliance analytics into a modern enterprise interface.

Designed as a realistic food safety management workspace rather than a basic inspection table, this example is suitable for regulatory compliance platforms, food manufacturing systems, restaurant management portals, warehouse quality assurance applications, and enterprise audit solutions.

## Features

- Enterprise food safety operations dashboard

- Executive food safety KPI cards
- Temperature compliance monitoring

- Facility inspection scoring
- Interactive inspection checklist
- Inspector activity timeline
- Animated compliance score visualization
