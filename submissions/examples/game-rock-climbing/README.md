# 🧗 Rock Climbing

A rock climbing simulation game where you climb a vertical wall by grabbing handholds. Choose left and right handhold positions, hold and release to climb up, and manage your grip strength. Fall and you lose progress!

## 🎮 How to Play

1. Click **"Start Climbing"** to begin
2. Use **Arrow Keys** to grab holds:
   - **←** – Grab a hold on the left
   - **→** – Grab a hold on the right
   - Press again to release a hold
   - **Space** – Jump (to reach higher holds)
3. **Alternate** between left and right holds to climb up
4. **Manage your grip strength** – it drains while holding
5. **Reach the top** to win!

## ⌨️ Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `←` | Grab/Release Left Hold |
| `→` | Grab/Release Right Hold |
| `Space` | Jump |
| `R` | Reset Game |

## ✨ Features

- 🧗 **Realistic Climbing Mechanics** – Grab, hold, release, and climb
- 🎯 **Two-Hand System** – Use left and right holds independently
- 💪 **Grip Strength** – Manages stamina; drains while holding
- 📊 **Height Tracking** – See how high you've climbed
- 🏆 **Best Height** – Saved in browser local storage
- 🎨 **Beautiful Visuals** – Gradient backgrounds, glowing holds
- 📱 **Responsive Design** – Works on desktop and mobile
- 🖱️ **Touch Support** – Tap left/right side of screen to grab

## 🎯 Goal

Climb to the top of the wall! Each hold you grab pulls you up. Alternate between left and right to make progress. Watch your grip strength!

## 📊 Stats

- **Height** – Current climbing height in meters
- **Best Height** – Your highest climb, saved locally
- **Grip Strength** – Your remaining grip percentage (drains while holding, regenerates when resting)
