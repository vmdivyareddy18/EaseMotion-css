Accessibility Guidance (WCAG 2.1)

Success Criterion 1.4.3

Contrast Minimums

Applying brightness, contrast, grayscale, or invert filters directly to elements containing text can easily reduce the contrast ratio below the required 4.5:1 minimum. Ensure that text remains highly legible against filters or apply graphical effects primarily to images/decorations instead of core paragraph content blocks.

Reduced Motion

Seizure Prevention (2.3.1)

Rapidly flashing color shifts, hue rotations, or sharp contrast invert sequences can cause seizures for users with photosensitive epilepsy. Do not animate filters at speeds greater than 3 flashes per second.
