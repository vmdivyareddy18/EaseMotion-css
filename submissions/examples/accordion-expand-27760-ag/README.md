# Accordion Expand/Collapse Max-Height Utilities

A collapsible accordion group demonstrating height expansions using max-height transitions.

---

## What is Included

1. **`demo.html`**: A showcase dashboard hosting multiple accordion panels, click handlers, and arrow indicator tags.
2. **`style.css`**: Content inner formatting, chevron rotations, max-height variables, and reduced motion settings.

---

## Verification Steps

1. Open `demo.html` in a web browser.
2. Click **Section 1: Details & Info** — confirm the panel expands downward with a smooth transition.
3. Click **Section 2: Rules & Policies** — confirm Section 1 collapses and Section 2 expands smoothly.
4. Verify that when expanded, the arrow indicators rotate 180 degrees.
