# Immigration Law Firm Template
A formal, authoritative, and trust-centered template for immigration law firms, global mobility consultancies, and visa-processing legal services.

- **Design**: Uses a "Legal-Prestige" palette with law-navy deep blues, trust-gold accents, and clean, structured layouts to communicate reliability, experience, and global reach.
- **Use Case**: Corporate immigration law firms, family visa consultancy services, and international relocation legal practices.
