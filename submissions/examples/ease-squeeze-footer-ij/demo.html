Squeeze Footer

Scroll down to see the footer squeeze effect in action.

The footer is sticky at the bottom. As you scroll past the main content, the footer squeezes — its height reduces and inner links fade into view.

This creates a parallax-like reveal where the footer compresses as content pushes past it.

Keep scrolling until the footer is fully compressed and all links are visible.
