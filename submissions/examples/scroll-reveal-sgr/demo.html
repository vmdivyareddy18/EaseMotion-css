Fades & Slide Entries

🔼

Fade Up (Default)

Fades and floats vertically into place. This is the default reveal pattern used on modern landing pages.

◀️

Slide Left

Translates from the left side of the viewport, creating a dynamic horizontal entrance vector.

▶️

Slide Right

Translates from the right side of the viewport, useful for asymmetrical grid entry alignments.

Zoom & 3D Transformations

🔍

Scale Zoom Reveal

Gently scales up from 85% to 100%. Ideal for hero features, pricing cards, and image focus reveals.

📦

3D Flip Perspective

Rotates forward along the 3D X-axis from a 1000px perspective plane for a dramatic depth entrance.
