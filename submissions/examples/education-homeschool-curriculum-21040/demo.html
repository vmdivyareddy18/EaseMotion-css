Education is an atmosphere, a discipline, a life.

We believe children are born persons, not empty vessels to be filled. Our curriculum uses "living books" instead of dry textbooks, prioritizing ideas over mere facts.

  • Short, focused lessons (15-30 mins) to build habit of attention
  • Literature-rich learning through stories and biographies
  • Nature study to foster observation and appreciation
  • Family-style learning combining subjects for multiple ages
