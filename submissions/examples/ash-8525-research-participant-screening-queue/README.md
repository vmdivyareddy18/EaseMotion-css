# Research Participant Screening Queue

A high-fidelity **Research Participant Screening Queue** built entirely with HTML and CSS.

## Overview

This example demonstrates an enterprise clinical research operations dashboard for managing participant recruitment and screening workflows. It presents fictional participant references across **Eligible**, **Needs Review**, **Waitlisted**, **Scheduled**, and **Ineligible** states while combining enrollment analytics, screening checklists, recruitment timelines, and study metrics into a modern enterprise interface.

Designed as a realistic study operations workspace rather than a simple recruitment table, this example can be adapted for clinical trial management systems, research recruitment platforms, healthcare operations dashboards, and academic research portals.
