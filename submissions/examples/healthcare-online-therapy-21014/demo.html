World-class care from licensed professionals.

Finding the right therapist is the most important part of your journey. That's why we maintain the highest standards for our provider network.

  • Fully Licensed: Every provider is a licensed, trained, experienced, and accredited psychologist (PhD / PsyD), marriage and family therapist (LMFT), clinical social worker (LCSW), or licensed professional counselor (LPC).
  • Vetted rigorously: Only 15% of therapists who apply to Haven are accepted into our network.
  • Experienced: All providers have a Master's Degree or Doctorate Degree in their field, with at least 3 years and 1,000 hours of hands-on experience.

Therapy tailored to you

There is no one-size-fits-all approach to mental health. Our therapists utilize evidence-based practices tailored to your specific goals.

🧠

Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT)

Identify and change negative thought patterns and behaviors. Highly effective for anxiety and depression.

🌊

Dialectical Behavior Therapy (DBT)

Learn skills to manage intense emotions, improve relationships, and practice mindfulness.

🗣️

Psychodynamic (Talk Therapy)

Explore how past experiences and unconscious thoughts influence your current behavior and feelings.

👁️

EMDR

A specialized approach to alleviate the distress associated with traumatic memories (available via video).
