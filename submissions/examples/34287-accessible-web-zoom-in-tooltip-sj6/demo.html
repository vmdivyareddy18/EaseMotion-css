Reading Tools

Every control below explains itself: hover it, or press Tab to reach it, and a high-contrast description zooms gently into view. The same information reaches pointer, keyboard, and screen-reader users.

Larger Text Scales the article text up to 200% without breaking the layout or requiring horizontal scrolling. High Contrast Switches to a dark-on-light theme measured at WCAG AAA (7:1) for body copy. Line Focus Dims everything except the paragraph you are reading, helping readers with dyslexia or low vision hold their place.
