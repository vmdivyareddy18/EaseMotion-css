Program Path

Online MBA Program Roadmap

Our rigorous 12-month program is divided into three comprehensive, practical phases.

1

Phase 1: Analytical Foundations

Build critical skills in financial accounting, statistics, quantitative microeconomics, and strategic team leadership. Includes weekly case analysis groups and interactive simulation modules.

  • • Corporate Financial Reporting
  • • Data Analysis & Statistical Modeling
  • • Managerial Economics & Incentives
2

Phase 2: Strategic Core & Tracks

Specialize in your chosen career path. Deep dive into advanced pricing models, capital budgeting, search engine economics, digital analytics, startup organization, and venture capital financing.

  • • Advanced Corporate Finance (Finance Track)
  • • Growth Marketing Attribution (Marketing Track)
  • • Startup Law & Venture Funding (Entrepreneurship Track)
3

Phase 3: Executive Capstone

Apply your learning to a live business situation. Students either design a venture and pitch it to real VCs, draft a comprehensive brand turnaround strategy for an enterprise, or engineer a complex M&A buyout model.

  • • Executive Board Simulation
  • • Strategic Capstone Thesis
  • • Partner/Investor Presentation
