Brewing Guide

Steep with precision, sip with ease.

A beautifully paced ritual begins with water, timing, and attention to the leaf.

Step 1

Water Temperature

Keep delicate leaves between 75C and 85C, and fuller teas between 90C and 96C.

Step 2

Steeping Time

Green and white teas bloom in 2 to 3 minutes, while black and oolong reveal depth in 3 to 5.

Step 3

Flavor Notes

Look for vegetal sweetness, toasted warmth, floral lift, or honeyed texture as the cup opens.
