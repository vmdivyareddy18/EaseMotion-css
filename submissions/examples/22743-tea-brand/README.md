# Issue #22743 - Premium Tea Brand Landing Page

## Project overview

This submission delivers a production-style premium tea brand landing page built entirely with HTML and CSS for `Issue #22743`. The design blends Japanese tea house calm, modern cafe polish, and luxury wellness-brand spacing using a warm cream palette, matcha and forest greens, soft gold accents, rounded glassmorphism cards, and CSS-only hero artwork.

## Folder contents

This folder contains exactly three files:

- `demo.html`
- `style.css`
- `README.md`

## Implemented sections

- Hero with CSS-only decorative tea artwork, headline, subheading, and dual CTAs
- Tea collections for Green Tea, Black Tea, Herbal, Oolong, and White Tea
- Tea sourcing origins cards for Japan, India, China, Sri Lanka, and Taiwan
- Brewing guide timeline for water temperature, steeping time, and flavor notes
- Premium subscription section with benefits and pricing cards
- Reviews section with testimonial cards and ratings
- Footer with newsletter form, quick links, social placeholders, and contact details

## Responsive behaviour

- Desktop: two-column hero, multi-column product and content grids, spacious premium layout
- Tablet: stacked major sections where needed, preserved card spacing and readable typography
- Mobile: single-column cards, compact hero art scaling, full-width newsletter action, simplified navigation wrap

## How to open demo.html

Open `demo.html` directly in any modern browser. It references the repo-root `easemotion.css` with a relative path and the local `style.css`, so no build step, JavaScript, or external assets are required.
