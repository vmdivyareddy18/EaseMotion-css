ease-miss

Erratic horizontal jitter simulating a near-miss reaction — more chaotic and faster than .ease-shake. Uses irregular translateX distances with non-uniform timing to feel unpredictable. Good for wrong answers, failed validation, and game misses.

Default trigger

Near miss!
