Design Spec: CONTACT SHEET

Ease Photo Gallery Masonry

A quiet, photographer-focused contact sheet display. Each frame carries a custom monospace tag stamp and reveals the image's description on focus or hover. Operates with zero third-party dependencies, featuring full keyboard accessibility and screen-reader optimizations.
