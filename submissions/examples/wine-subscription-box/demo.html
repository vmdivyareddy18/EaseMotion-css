Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers

How is shipping handled in hot weather?

We use specialized climate-controlled logistics trucks and heat-shield insulation liners. If regional temperatures are extreme, we hold the wines in temp-regulated storage and notify you before dispatch to protect bottle integrity.

Can I customize my box to red or white only?

Yes! Once your subscription is active, you can log in to your Vignette profile and toggle your preferences to Red Only, White Only, or our standard Mixed Box at no additional charge.

How do I cancel or pause my subscription?

We believe in complete freedom. You can pause, skip, or cancel your monthly membership with a single click inside your dashboard settings before the billing date of each month. No commitments, no fees.
