Animated Copy-Confirmation Button Collection

Four reusable confirmation animations for any copy button — works on code, emails, links, or coupon codes. All animation is CSS; the only JavaScript is the clipboard call itself and toggling .is-copied.

ease-copy-morph

Icon morphs from a copy glyph into a checkmark on click.

ease-copy-pulse

Button briefly scale-pulses and flashes green on click.

ease-copy-label-swap

Label crossfades from "Copy" to "Copied!" and back.

ease-copy-ring

Expanding ripple-style ring burst confirms the click registered.
