Shape Outside

Defines a shape for text to flow around using circles, ellipses, or polygons.

CSS Property: shape-outside

Variants Demo

shape-outside: circle(40% at 60% 50%) Shape Outside variant
shape-outside: ellipse(40% 50% at 40% 50%) Shape Outside variant
shape-outside: polygon(0 0, 100% 0, 100% 100%) Shape Outside variant

Usage

<div class="shape-outside">Content here</div>
