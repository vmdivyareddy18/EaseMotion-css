The Bite Street Story

It started in a backyard in 2024 with a rusty flat-top grill and an obsession with creating the perfect crust on a smash burger.

Two years later, we quit our day jobs, bought a used delivery step-van, painted it bright yellow, and hit the streets. We believe in simple ingredients, high heat, and food that makes you need extra napkins.
