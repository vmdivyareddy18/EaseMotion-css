Accessibility Guidance (WCAG 2.1)

      

        

          
Success Criterion 1.4.10

          
Reflow & Content Clipping

          

            When utilizing `flex-shrink-0`, items will refuse to wrap or shrink
            even under high levels of browser zoom. Ensure that text blocks
            containing paragraphs never utilize `flex-shrink-0` to avoid
            horizontal layout breaking, which fails WCAG 2.1 zoom requirements.
          

        


        

          
Responsive Wrapping

          
Flexible Grid Sizing

          

            In horizontal layouts, configure media queries to override active
            growth parameters on mobile screens (e.g. setting `sm-flex-grow-1`
            or `sm-flex-shrink-1` alongside `flex-direction: column`). This
            preserves clear document readability.
