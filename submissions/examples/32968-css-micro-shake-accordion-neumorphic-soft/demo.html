Neumorphic Soft Accordion

A soft-extruded UI accordion triggering tactile micro-shake hover animations and transitioning into smooth recessed containers when active.

Neumorphic (Soft UI) styling uses dual drop shadows (one light, one dark) to make flat elements look extruded or recessed from the parent canvas, mimicking real-world plastic-molded physical interfaces.
When hovered or keyboard focused, a high-frequency horizontal keyframe animation shakes the panel subtly, suggesting a physical, tactile click response.
Neumorphism is often criticized for low accessibility. To prevent this, we use clear dark slate text, high contrast outlines on focus-visible inputs, and responsive layout structures.
