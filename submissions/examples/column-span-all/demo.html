Using column-span:all on headings creates a structure that is both visually appealing and functionally clear. It mirrors the layout patterns found in print magazines, newspapers, and academic papers.

This technique works well for blog posts, documentation, and any content that benefits from columnar organization with clear section headers.

Conclusion

Column-span:all is a powerful tool for editorial design on the web. It bridges the gap between print typography and digital flexibility, giving designers control over both flow and structure.
