How TickTock works

Three simple steps to better time management and accurate billing.

⏱️
Step 1

Track your time

Hit the play button, type what you're doing, and get to work. Or add time manually at the end of the day if you prefer.

📊
Step 2

Analyze performance

See exactly where your team's time is going with beautiful, visual reports. Identify bottlenecks and improve estimations.

🧾
Step 3

Bill clients

Turn your tracked hours into professional invoices in one click. Set custom billable rates per project or per team member.

Everything you need, nothing you don't

🔔

Idle Detection

Left your timer running while you went to lunch? We'll spot the inactivity and ask if you want to keep or discard that time.

📱

Cross-Platform

Start a timer on your Mac, stop it on your phone. Everything syncs perfectly via our web, desktop, and mobile apps.

💰

Project Budgets

Set a time or financial budget for a project and get alerts when you're approaching the limit.

👥

Team Permissions

Control who sees what. Hide billable rates from contractors while giving managers access to project reports.

📅

Calendar View

Connect your Google or Outlook calendar to see your meetings and easily drag-and-drop them into time entries.

🏷️

Tags & Labels

Organize your data exactly how you want. Tag entries to easily pull specific data across different projects later.
