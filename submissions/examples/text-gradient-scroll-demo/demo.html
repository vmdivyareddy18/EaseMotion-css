Scroll-Driven Text Gradient Demo

The headings below animate their gradient position as they scroll through the viewport, using animation-timeline: view(). Scroll down slowly to see each heading's gradient shift from left to right as it enters and crosses the viewport.

💡 Browser support: Chrome/Edge 115+ animate via scroll position. Firefox and Safari fall back to the existing static .ease-gradient-text look — no broken or jumpy behavior, just no scroll-tied motion.
