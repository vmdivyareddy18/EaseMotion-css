EaseMotion Expandable Card

A pure CSS expanding card using the powerful `:checked` state trick.

Mountain Retreat

Experience the ultimate getaway in the heart of the mountains. This expanded view is powered entirely by CSS. No Javascript was used to open or close this modal!

The trick relies on binding labels to a hidden checkbox, and utilizing the :checked pseudo-class paired with the general sibling selector ~ to trigger hardware-accelerated transforms and opacity shifts.
