# Border Glow – Glowing outline on focus/hover

> A production-ready border glow component that adds a beautiful glowing outline effect on focus and hover states with customizable intensity, color, and toggle controls. Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—no dependencies required.

---

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Glowing outline** – Beautiful glow on focus/hover
- ✅ **3 intensity levels** – Low, Medium, High
- ✅ **Multiple elements** – Inputs, buttons, cards
- ✅ **Toggle glow** – Enable/disable effect
- ✅ **Color cycling** – Cycle through preset colors
- ✅ **Keyboard shortcuts** – Ctrl+G (toggle), Ctrl+1-3 (intensity), Ctrl+C (color)
- ✅ **6 color variants** – Blue, Green, Red, Purple, Gold, Pink
- ✅ **Responsive** – Adapts to any screen size
- ✅ **Light/Dark mode** – Auto-adjusts to system preference
- ✅ **Zero dependencies** – No libraries or frameworks
