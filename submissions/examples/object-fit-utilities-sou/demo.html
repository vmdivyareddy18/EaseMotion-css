Object-Fit & Object-Position Utilities

Pure CSS demo showing how images and videos behave inside fixed containers.

1. The 5 object-fit Values

Same image — 1200×800px — placed inside a 100% × 140px container. The checkered background reveals empty space.

.fit-fill

Stretches to fill — distorts aspect ratio.

.fit-contain

Fits inside — keeps ratio, may leave gaps.

.fit-cover

Fills container — keeps ratio, crops edges.

.fit-none

Original size — ignores container.

.fit-scale-down

Smaller of none or contain.
