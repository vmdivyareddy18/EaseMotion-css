clip-rule: nonzero vs evenodd

The clip-rule property (and fill-rule) controls how intersecting paths determine what is inside or outside a shape.

nonzero

Winding direction determines fill; overlapping areas with same direction fill.

evenodd

Parity-based fill; alternating intersections invert fill.

How It Works

  • nonzero: Counts winding direction. Odd crossings = inside.
  • evenodd: Counts any crossing. Odd parity = inside, even = outside.
  • Used in clip-rule for SVG clip paths and fill-rule for SVG shapes.
