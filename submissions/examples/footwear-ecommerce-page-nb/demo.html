CloudFoam+ technology delivers responsive, weightless cushioning that absorbs impact and returns energy with every stride, reducing fatigue during long runs.

Breathable Knit Upper

Precision-engineered FlyKnit weave creates a sock-like fit with targeted ventilation zones, keeping your feet cool and dry even during intense training sessions.

Energy-Return Midsoles

ReboundX foam capsules in the midsole capture and release energy with each footstrike, giving you a spring-loaded sensation that propels you forward.

Fit Recommendations

  • • If between sizes, choose the larger size for optimal comfort
  • • Running shoes should have a thumb's width of space at the toe
  • • Basketball shoes fit snugger — consider half size up
  • • Outdoor boots accommodate thicker socks, go half size up

Width Guidance

Most STRIDE models are available in Narrow (B), Medium (D), Wide (2E), and Extra Wide (4E) widths. Check individual product pages for availability by width.
