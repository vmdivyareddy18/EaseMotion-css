Ingredient sourcing story

From hillside growers to your bedside table

We partner with regenerative herb farms in Oregon, Vermont, and British Columbia, selecting botanicals at peak aroma and drying them slowly to preserve their natural character.

Every batch is documented with harvest season, tasting notes, and blending guidance so your wellness ritual feels intentional, transparent, and easy to return to.
