Accessibility & Usage

Requirements

  • Wrap front/back content in .ease-flip-card-inner
  • Use .ease-flip-card-front and .ease-flip-card-back as direct children
  • Add tabindex="0" and role="button" for keyboard accessibility
  • Use .ease-flip-card-flip-x for vertical (X-axis) flip instead of horizontal
  • Respects prefers-reduced-motion: reduce — disables flip animation
