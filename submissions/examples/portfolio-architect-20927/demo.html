The Studio

Founded in 2012 by Julian Kahn, our studio operates at the intersection of critical regionalism and minimal abstraction. We approach architecture not merely as the enclosure of space, but as the deliberate organization of light, material, and human experience.

Based in Portland, OR, our small team of architects and designers takes on a limited number of commissions annually to maintain rigorous oversight on every detail.
