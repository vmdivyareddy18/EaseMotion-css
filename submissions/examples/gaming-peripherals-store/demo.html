Under the Hood

Pro Specs

Numbers that separate the amateurs from the professionals. No marketing fluff — just raw engineering data.

Max DPI

36,000

Nova Hero 2 optical sensor — industry-leading tracking accuracy at any sensitivity setting.

Polling Rate

8,000 Hz

Report every 0.125ms. At 8K polling, your movements are registered faster than a display frame at 240Hz.

Response Time

< 0.2ms

Click-to-signal latency under 0.2ms. In a 240Hz match, that's the difference between a hit and a miss.

Switch Type

Nova Red v3

50M-cycle rated linear switches. 45g actuation force. Factory pre-lubed for buttery smooth keystrokes.

Refresh Rate

240 Hz

IPS 2K panel, G-Sync Compatible, 1ms GtG. Every frame rendered crystal-clear with zero ghosting.

RGB Zones

16 Zones

16 individually addressable lighting zones with 16.8M colours. Sync with audio, CPU load, or game events.

Weight

58g

Honeycomb shell design. 58g without cable. Optimal for low-sens precision and high-sens flick shots.

Battery Life

70 Hours

70 hours at 1K polling. 45 hours at 4K. 25 hours at full 8K. Quick-charge: 15min = 10 hours.
