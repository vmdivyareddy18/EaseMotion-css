# Plasma Modal (`plasma-modal-ksk`)

1. What does this do?  
Displays a SaaS upgrade modal with three drifting plasma glow blobs animating inside the card, giving a living energy effect. The card scales and fades in from below on open.

2. How is it used?  
Pair a hidden `.plasma-toggle` checkbox with a `.plasma-trigger-label` button. The `.plasma-backdrop` and `.plasma-modal` activate on `:checked`. Labels on the close button and action buttons uncheck the toggle to dismiss.

3. Why is it useful?  
Delivers a high-impact SaaS upsell or alert modal with ambient plasma atmosphere using only CSS keyframe animations and the checkbox hack — zero JavaScript.
