Our Academic Programs

Tailored curriculum for every age and skill level.

Lincoln-Douglas & Public Forum

Develop rigorous logical argumentation, research skills, and the ability to think on your feet in competitive 1-on-1 and 2-on-2 debate formats.

  • Evidence analysis
  • Cross-examination tactics
  • Case construction

Speech & Interpretation

Master the art of presentation. From Original Oratory to Dramatic Interpretation, students learn vocal control, blocking, and persuasive delivery.

  • Extemporaneous speaking
  • Humorous & dramatic acting
  • Speechwriting

Model United Nations

Step into the shoes of global diplomats. Students tackle real-world international crises, draft resolutions, and learn complex negotiation strategies.

  • Global policy research
  • Parliamentary procedure
  • Collaborative negotiation
