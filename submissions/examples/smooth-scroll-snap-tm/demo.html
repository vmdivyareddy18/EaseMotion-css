Section 1: Scroll Snap Fundamentals

CSS scroll-snap locks the scroll position to snap points after each scroll gesture, creating a polished, app-like experience without JavaScript.

How It Works

The scroll-snap-type property controls axis and strictness. Combined with scroll-snap-align on children, you define where the viewport locks after each scroll gesture.

axisy mandatory
alignstart / center / end
strictnessmandatory vs proximity
