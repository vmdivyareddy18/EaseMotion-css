ease-scroll-reveal

Reveal elements as they enter the viewport — using modern animation-timeline: view() with a tiny IntersectionObserver fallback. Scroll down to see all 10 reveal variants in action.

↓ Scroll to begin

1. Directional Reveals

Slide in from any direction as the element enters the viewport.

.ease-reveal-up

Slides up from below.

.ease-reveal-down

Drops in from above.

.ease-reveal-left

Slides in from the right.

.ease-reveal-right

Slides in from the left.

2. Zoom & Flip Effects

Add depth and personality with scale and 3D rotation reveals.

.ease-reveal-zoom-in

Scales up into place.

.ease-reveal-zoom-out

Scales down into place.

.ease-reveal-flip-x

Flips on the X axis.

.ease-reveal-flip-y

Flips on the Y axis.

3. Blur & Rotate

Cinematic reveals with motion blur and tilt.

.ease-reveal-blur

Sharpens into focus.

.ease-reveal-rotate

Rotates and scales in.

4. Speed Variants

Add .ease-fast or .ease-slow to change the reveal duration.

default

1100ms — graceful reveal.
