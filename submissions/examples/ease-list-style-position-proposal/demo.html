List Style Position Utilities

Typographic list controls proposed for core/utilities.css.

These utility classes control whether list markers (bullets/numbers) appear inside or outside the content flow of the list item.

.list-outside (Default)

The marker sits outside the list item's content box. When text wraps, it aligns with the start of the text, not the marker.

  • Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.
  • Ut enim ad minim veniam, quis nostrud exercitation ullamco laboris.
  • Duis aute irure dolor in reprehenderit in voluptate.

.list-inside

The marker sits inside the list item's content box. When text wraps, it wraps directly under the marker.

  • Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet, consectetur adipiscing elit, sed do eiusmod tempor incididunt ut labore et dolore magna aliqua.
  • Ut enim ad minim veniam, quis nostrud exercitation ullamco laboris.
  • Duis aute irure dolor in reprehenderit in voluptate.
