Capture Pipeline Pipeline

Systematic workflow mapping the carbon capture, extraction, and mineralization lifecycle.

01

Air Intake

Industrial fan arrays pull ambient atmospheric air through molecular capture grids.

02

Chemical Binding

Carbon molecules selectively bind to solid amine solid matrix filters.

03

Thermal Release

Steam heating at 100°C releases high-purity carbon gas from filters.

04

Fluid Compress

Carbon gas is compressed into a supercritical fluid state for pipeline transit.

05

Deep Mineralization

Supercritical CO₂ is injected into deep basalt formations, turning to stone in 2 years.
