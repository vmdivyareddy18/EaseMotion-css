# Eyes Follow Cursor – Eyes on screen track mouse movement

> A production-ready eyes follow cursor component where animated eyes on screen track mouse movement with customizable size, color, speed, and styles. Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—no dependencies required.

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Real-time eye tracking** – Eyes follow cursor movement
- ✅ **Smooth animation** – Fluid eye movement with easing
- ✅ **4 eye styles** – Normal, Cartoon, Minimal, Glow
- ✅ **Customizable** – Speed, eye size, pupil size, color
- ✅ **Toggle eyes** – Enable/disable tracking
- ✅ **Keyboard shortcuts** – Space (toggle), R (reset), S (style)
- ✅ **4 color variants** – Ocean, Sunset, Gold, Dream
- ✅ **Responsive** – Adapts to any screen size
- ✅ **Light/Dark mode** – Auto-adjusts to system preference
- ✅ **Zero dependencies** – No libraries or frameworks
