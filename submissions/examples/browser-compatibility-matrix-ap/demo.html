Fallback & Polyfill Recommendations

      

        

          
Houdini Variable Polyfill

          

            For legacy browsers that do not support custom property transitions via @property,
            use the Houdini properties polyfill script to enable transitions on angles and gradients.
          

        


        

          
Scroll Timeline Polyfill

          

            To ensure scroll-timeline transitions (animation-timeline) work in browsers without native support,
            load the scroll-timeline polyfill in your project's index template.
