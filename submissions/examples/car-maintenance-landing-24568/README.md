# Car Maintenance App Template
A robust, utility-focused, and high-performance landing page template for vehicle maintenance apps, automotive record-keeping tools, and driver assistance platforms.

- **Design**: Uses a "Precision-Automotive" palette with track-gray industrial backgrounds, rev-red alert highlights, and clean card-based layouts to communicate efficiency, reliability, and technical oversight.
- **Use Case**: Car maintenance tracking apps, automotive service logbooks, and driver fleet-management tools.
