# Quantum Computing Operations Center

**Phase 402** - Research-grade platform orchestrating quantum workloads, resource scheduling, and experiment monitoring for quantum computing research facilities.

## Overview

This command center provides comprehensive management of quantum computing resources including qubit allocation, experiment scheduling, and performance monitoring. Researchers can visualize quantum circuit execution, analyze noise profiles, and optimize resource utilization across quantum processors.

## Features

### Quantum Cluster Overview
Real-time dashboard displaying available qubits, running experiments, quantum fidelity metrics, and queue status with live system health indicators.

### Qubit Utilization
Interactive visualization of quantum processor topology showing qubit connectivity, error rates, and current allocation across all quantum circuits.

### Experiment Timeline
Comprehensive experiment tracking with circuit visualization, execution history, and performance benchmarking for reproducible quantum research.

### Error Correction Center
Real-time quantum error correction monitoring with syndrome detection, gate fidelity analysis, and decoherence time tracking.

### Executive Research Dashboard
High-level metrics for research leadership including experiment success rates, publication metrics, and quantum advantage assessments.
