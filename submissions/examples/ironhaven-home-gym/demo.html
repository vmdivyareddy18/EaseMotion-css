Blueprint to Iron

How to plan your space

Building a home gym can be overwhelming. Follow our 3-step blueprint to ensure you get exactly what you need for your space and training style.

1

Measure Your Footprint

Account for ceiling height and barbell clearance. We recommend at least 10x10ft for a full rack setup.

2

Protect Your Floors

Never skip horse stall mats or high-density rubber tiles. They protect your foundation and your weights.

3

Select Core Movers First

Start with a rack, bench, and barbell. Machines and accessories can wait. Focus on the compound basics.
