Filter Brightness Utilities

Demonstrates the visual effect of brightness filters. Hover over each card to toggle between modified and baseline states.

Dim (50%)

Atmospheric Glow

This card is dimmed to 50% brightness. Hovering increases brightness back to 100%.

Normal (100%)

Baseline Balance

This card maintains baseline 100% brightness. Hovering has no modification.

Bright (150%)

High Contrast

This card is boosted to 150% brightness. Hovering dims it back down to 100%.
