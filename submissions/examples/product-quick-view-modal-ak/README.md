# Product Quick View Modal

## What does this do?
A product grid with quick-view buttons that open a modal overlay showing detailed product information, rating stars, and an add-to-cart button.

## How is it used?
Open demo.html in a browser. Click "Quick View" on any product card to open the modal with full details. Click the close button, overlay, or press Escape to dismiss.

## Why is this useful?
Demonstrates an e-commerce quick-view modal pattern with a responsive product grid, modal overlay with backdrop dismiss, and keyboard support.

## Tech Stack
- HTML
- CSS (no frameworks)
- Vanilla JavaScript

## Preview
Open demo.html directly in your browser.
