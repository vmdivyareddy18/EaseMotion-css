CSS text-decoration Animation

Native text-decoration-thickness, text-underline-offset, and text-decoration-color are all animatable — no pseudo-elements or transforms required.

Thickness — hover & pulse

Hover to grow underline thickness Pulsing thickness via @keyframes

text-decoration-thickness accepts px, em, or from-font. Transitions and animations work natively — no pseudo-element needed.

Offset — float & spring

Underline floating away Hover for spring offset jump

text-underline-offset moves the underline away from the text baseline. Pair with cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1) for a spring overshoot effect.

Color — cycle & reveal

Cycling underline color Hover to reveal color

text-decoration-color interpolates between color values in both transition and @keyframes. The text color stays untouched.

Wavy style — pulse & hover

Wavy underline pulsing Hover to animate wavy decoration

text-decoration-style: wavy combined with animated text-decoration-thickness and text-underline-offset gives a dynamic squiggly underline effect.
