# Email Subscription Card

## What does this do?

Provides a modern email newsletter subscription component with premium styling, responsive layout, and elegant interactions.

---

## How is it used?

Open `demo.html` directly in any browser.

```html
<div class="card">

    <form>

        <input
            type="email"
            placeholder="Enter your email"
        >

        <button>
            Subscribe
        </button>

    </form>

</div>
```

---

## Why is it useful?

Newsletter signup forms are one of the most common UI elements across landing pages, SaaS products, portfolios, and blogs. This component provides a polished standalone example that aligns with EaseMotion CSS's minimal and animation-first philosophy.
