# Farm-to-Table Restaurant

A sustainable restaurant landing page built entirely with **EaseMotion CSS utility classes**.

This example demonstrates a modern farm-to-table dining experience emphasizing seasonal ingredients, local sourcing and environmental responsibility.

---

## Features

### Hero Section

* Seasonal dining introduction
* Farm imagery
* CTA buttons
* Entrance animations

### Seasonal Menu

Featured dishes include:

* Spring Harvest Salad
* Roasted Garden Bowl
* Tomato Burrata

Interactive menu cards utilize EaseMotion hover effects.

### Partner Farms

Showcases local suppliers:

* Organic farms
* Heritage grain producers
* Artisan dairies

### Sustainability Commitment

Highlights:

* Local sourcing
* Reduced food waste
* Seasonal ingredients
* Responsible farming practices

### Chef Story

Introduces the culinary philosophy behind the restaurant.

### Reservation CTA

Encourages guests to book a dining experience.

### Reviews

Guest testimonials provide social proof.

### Footer

Brand identity and restaurant information.
