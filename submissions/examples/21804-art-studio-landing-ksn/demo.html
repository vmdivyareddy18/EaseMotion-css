Our Mediums

Creative Class Offerings

We guide artists step-by-step from fundamental principles to advanced exhibition portfolio works.

Painting & Color Theory

Learn mixing, color theory, brushwork, and light control with acrylics, watercolor, and oil paints. Includes canvas preparation.

Drawing & Sketching

Master perspective, anatomy, portraiture, shading, and life drawing using graphite, charcoal, ink, and pastel techniques.

Wheel Throwing & Clay

Focus on throwing pots on the wheel, hand-building, glazing and kiln firing. Perfect for creating functional ceramic housewares.
