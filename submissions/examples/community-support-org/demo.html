PrideBridge Community is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit providing affirming counseling, housing support, legal aid, and a vibrant community for LGBTQ+ individuals and allies. No one walks alone.

Our Services

Comprehensive, affirming care designed by and for the LGBTQ+ community.

📚

Affirming Counseling

One-on-one and group therapy with licensed counselors who are trained in LGBTQ+ affirming care, gender identity exploration, and trauma-informed practices.

Learn more →
🏠

Housing Support

Emergency shelter referrals, transitional housing programs, and roommate matching for LGBTQ+ youth and adults facing housing instability or discrimination.

Learn more →
⚖️

Legal Aid

Free legal clinics for name and gender marker changes, immigration assistance, employment discrimination, family law, and housing rights advocacy.

Learn more →
🌱

Youth Programs

Weekly drop-in groups, mentorship matching, scholarship support, and leadership development for LGBTQ+ youth ages 13–24 in a safe, supervised space.

Learn more →
🏥

Health & Wellness

PrEP navigation, gender-affirming care referrals, support groups, yoga, meditation, and nutrition workshops tailored to our community's needs.

Learn more →
🤝

Crisis Support

24/7 peer crisis line, de-escalation support, safety planning, and connections to emergency services — always staffed by trained LGBTQ+ responders.

Learn more →
