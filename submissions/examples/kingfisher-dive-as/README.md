# Kingfisher Dive

A pure CSS and HTML kingfisher plunging from its branch into the water after a fish, then rising back to its perch.

## What it shows

- The whole dive choreographed on one timeline: the bird tips, folds its wings, plunges through the surface, and climbs back out
- A crown splash ring and a spray of droplets fired at the moment of entry, each droplet carrying its own direction in a custom property
- Electric blue plumage from a layered sheen and a fine repeating linear gradient that flashes on the stoop
- A fish bolting away and the surface tensing as the bird hits
- No images, no JavaScript, no external dependencies

## Usage

Open `demo.html` in any modern browser.

## Accessibility

All motion stops under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.
