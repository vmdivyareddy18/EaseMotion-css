# Social Share Button – Expands into share options on hover

> A sleek, production-ready social share button that expands into a dropdown with share options on hover or click. Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—no dependencies required.

---

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Expands on hover** – Smooth dropdown animation
- ✅ **Click mode** – Toggleable for mobile/accessibility
- ✅ **Multiple platforms** – Facebook, Twitter, LinkedIn, WhatsApp, Email
- ✅ **Copy link** – Copy URL to clipboard
- ✅ **Keyboard accessible** – Arrow keys, Enter, Escape
- ✅ **Responsive** – Adapts to any screen size
- ✅ **Light/Dark mode** – Auto-adjusts to system preference
- ✅ **Zero dependencies** – No libraries or frameworks
- ✅ **Highly customizable** – Colors, URLs, messages, callbacks
