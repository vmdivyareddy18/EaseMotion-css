Table-cell vs Modern Flex

Table-cell

Works in older browsers. Requires wrapper with display: table and row with display: table-row. Vertical centering is straightforward.

Flexbox

Modern alternative. Fewer wrapper elements. Better gap and alignment control. align-items: stretch for equal height.
