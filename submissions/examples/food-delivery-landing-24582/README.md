# Local Food Delivery App Template
A high-energy, appetite-stimulating, and conversion-focused landing page template for food delivery apps, restaurant platforms, and local courier services.

- **Design**: Uses a "Fresh-Crave" palette with delivery-orange energizing backgrounds, fresh-green success accents, and clean card layouts designed to drive app installs and order frequency.
- **Use Case**: Food delivery apps, restaurant aggregation platforms, and local logistics solutions.
