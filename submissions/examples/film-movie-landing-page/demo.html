Synopsis

The Mission Parameters

In the year 2098, Earth’s biosphere is collapsing. A crew of elite scientists and pilots aboard the vessel Chronos are dispatched on a desperate voyage. Their objective: travel through a newly discovered wormhole on the outer edge of Saturn to retrieve data from an ancient interstellar beacon that may hold the key to temporal reconstruction.

Faced with relativistic time dilation, space anomalies, and internal clashes, the crew must choose between saving the future of humanity or returning to the families they left behind in the past.
