EaseScroll

A lightweight, native scroll-triggered transition animation library. Seamlessly pairs IntersectionObserver with customizable CSS transition utility classes.

Fade Scroll Effects

Smooth opacity transitions combined with subtle directional slides to direct attention.

🚀

Fade Up

Translates upward by 40px and fades in, creating a standard premium entrance.

class="ease-scroll ease-scroll-fade-up"
📥

Fade Down

Translates downward by 40px and fades in. Excellent for headers and alerts.

class="ease-scroll ease-scroll-fade-down"
👈

Fade Left

Fades in while translating from the right side, ideal for features on the left.

class="ease-scroll ease-scroll-fade-left"
👉

Fade Right

Fades in while translating from the left side, ideal for features on the right.

class="ease-scroll ease-scroll-fade-right"

Custom Configurations

Customize parameters per-element declaratively using data-attributes (duration, delay, threshold, repeat mode).

🔁

Repeatable Animation

This card resets its animation whenever you scroll away and triggers again when you scroll back!

data-ease-scroll-once="false"
⏳

Custom Slow & Delayed

Takes 1.2 seconds to complete and has a 300ms delay.

data-ease-scroll-duration="1200" data-ease-scroll-delay="300"
🎯

High Threshold (50%)

Will not animate until at least 50% of the card is visible in the viewport.

data-ease-scroll-threshold="0.5"
