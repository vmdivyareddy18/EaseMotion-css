ease-text-highlight-sweep

A highlighter marker sweep effect that glides across text on hover — stays highlighted after the sweep completes.

How It Works

A linear-gradient background is combined with background-size animated from 0% 100% to 100% 100%. On hover, the background-size grows to cover the full text width, creating a marker sweep effect. animation-fill-mode: forwards keeps the highlight visible. The highlight color is controlled via the --ease-highlight-color CSS variable.
