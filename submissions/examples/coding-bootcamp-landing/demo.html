Do I need coding experience to apply? +
No experience is required. We accept complete beginners. Our only requirement is motivation and the ability to commit to 40-50 hours per week for 16 weeks.
What is the hiring guarantee? +
If you complete the programme, follow the career curriculum, and do not receive a job offer within 180 days of graduation, we refund 100% of your tuition. No questions asked.
Is the programme online or in-person? +
CodeForge is fully remote-first. Live sessions run Mon-Fri 9am-6pm GMT. All sessions are recorded so you can catch up if you have a scheduling conflict.
