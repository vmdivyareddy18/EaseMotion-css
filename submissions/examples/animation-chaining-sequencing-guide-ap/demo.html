Key Orchestration Concepts

      

        
        

          
Shorthand Multi-Chaining

          

            To chain multiple animations on a single element, separate each declaration with a comma in the
            animation shorthand property. The browser executes them concurrently or sequentially
            depending on your animation-delay settings.
          

        


        
        

          
The Role of Fill-Mode "Both"

          

            When staggering sequences, elements with delays will flash in their fully loaded state before the animation begins.
            Setting animation-fill-mode: both forces the element to stick to its initial frame properties (e.g. opacity: 0) during the delay, and retain the final frame styles upon completion.
          

        


        
        

          
Sequencing via Offsets

          

            Sequencing across multiple items is done by adding progressive animation-delay values
            (e.g., 0.2s, 0.4s, 0.6s) to consecutive elements. This creates a logical flow, guiding the user's eye step-by-step.
