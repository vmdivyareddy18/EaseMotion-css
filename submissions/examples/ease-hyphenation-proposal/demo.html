Hyphenation Control

Typographic breaking controls proposed for core/utilities.css.

These utility classes control how words are hyphenated across line breaks, improving readability in narrow columns or for very long words.

.hyphens-none

Words are never hyphenated, even if they overflow their container.

This is an extraordinarilylongwordthatcannotbebroken and will just overflow if it doesn't fit on one line.

.hyphens-manual

Words are only hyphenated where explicitly indicated by an invisible &shy; (soft hyphen) character.

This is an extra­ordinarily­long­word­that­can­be­broken only at specific soft-hyphen points.

.hyphens-auto

The browser automatically hyphenates words according to its dictionary for the declared lang attribute.

This is an extraordinarily long word that might be automatically hyphenated by the browser's language dictionary when it reaches the edge of this extremely narrow container.
