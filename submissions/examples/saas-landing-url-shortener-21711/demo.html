More than just short links

A complete suite of tools for managing your digital presence and understanding your audience.

🔗

Custom Domains

Use your own domain name (like brand.link) to create branded short links that build trust and increase click-through rates.

📱

Dynamic QR Codes

Generate high-res, customizable QR codes. Change the destination URL anytime without having to print a new code.

📊

Advanced Analytics

Track clicks, geographic data, device types, and referrers in real-time. Export data to your favorite BI tools.

🎯

Link Retargeting

Add retargeting pixels (Facebook, Google, LinkedIn) directly to your short links to build custom audiences.

Team Collaboration
