# Mudskipper

A pure CSS and HTML mudskipper, the fish that walks on land, propping itself up on the mud flat and skipping forward on its pectoral fins.

## What it shows

- A crutching skip: the pectoral fins swing back on a stomp keyframe while the whole fish lunges forward, then settles
- A raised sail dorsal fin flashing up, cut from clip-path spikes with a striped repeating linear gradient
- Bulging periscope eyes on top of the head from stacked radial gradients, blinking now and then
- Wet mottled skin from hard stop radial gradients, over a mud flat with lapping water and spreading ripples
- No images, no JavaScript, no external dependencies

## Usage

Open `demo.html` in any modern browser.

## Accessibility

All motion stops under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.
