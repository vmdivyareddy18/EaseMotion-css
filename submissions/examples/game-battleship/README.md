# ⚓ Battleship

A classic Battleship game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Place your ships on a grid, take turns guessing coordinates to sink opponent's ships, and destroy the enemy fleet before they sink yours!

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Place your ships** – Click "Place Ships" and click on your board to place each ship
2. **Start the battle** – Click "Start Game" to begin
3. **Take turns** – Click on the AI board to attack
4. **Hit/Miss indicators** – Red for hits, white dots for misses
5. **Sink all ships** – Destroy the enemy fleet to win!

## ⌨️ Controls

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `Click` | Place ship / Attack |
| `P` | Place Ships / Rotate |
| `Space` | Start Game |
| `R` | Reset Game |

## ✨ Features

- ⚓ **Classic Battleship Gameplay** – Place ships, attack, sink
- 🎯 **5 Ship Types** – Carrier (5), Battleship (4), Cruiser (3), Submarine (3), Destroyer (2)
- 🤖 **AI Opponent** – Smart targeting with adjacency logic
- 🎨 **Visual Feedback** – Hit (red), Miss (white dot), Sunk (highlighted)
- 📊 **Stats Tracking** – Hit counts for both players
- 📱 **Responsive Design** – Works on desktop and mobile
- 🖱️ **Touch Support** – Play on mobile devices
- 🔄 **Ship Placement** – Drag-click to place, rotate with button
