The Anatomy of a Scent.

Like a symphony, a true fragrance reveals itself over time. Our compositions are structured in three distinct acts, ensuring a captivating evolution on the skin from the first spritz to the final whisper.

The Awakening (Top)

Volatile and bright. Citrus and light florals that capture attention instantly before gracefully yielding to the heart.

The Soul (Heart)

The true character of the fragrance. Rich florals, spices, and fruits that linger for hours.

The Memory (Base)

Deep, resonant, and intimate. Woods, musks, and resins that bind to the skin and last throughout the night.
