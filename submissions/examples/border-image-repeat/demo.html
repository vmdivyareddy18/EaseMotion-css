Border Image Repeat

Comparing stretch, repeat, round, and space

The Four Repeat Values

Stretch
border-image-repeat: stretch

Edge images are stretched to fill the side. No tiling; smooth but may distort.

Repeat
border-image-repeat: repeat

Edge images are tiled. Tiles may be clipped at edges if they don't fit evenly.

Round
border-image-repeat: round

Tiles are scaled to fit an integer number of repetitions. No clipping.

Space
border-image-repeat: space

Tiles are placed with equal gaps between them to fill the side.

Two-Axis Combinations

Stretch / Repeat
border-image-repeat: stretch repeat
Round / Space
border-image-repeat: round space
Repeat / Round
border-image-repeat: repeat round
Stretch / Space
border-image-repeat: stretch space
