drop-shadow() Motion Trail

Stack multiple filter: drop-shadow() layers with directional offsets that reverse with the motion — simulating a velocity-aware light trail.

Horizontal bounce — trailing shadow flips direction

At 0% the shadow offsets point right (trailing behind leftward motion). At 50% they flip left — matching the direction of travel.

Vertical oscillation — upward trail on descent

Three stacked drop-shadow() calls: tight near-shadow at high opacity, wider mid-shadow, and a large diffuse outer glow at low opacity.

Orbital — shadow direction rotates with the path

A second @keyframes rotates the shadow offset to stay behind the orbiting dot — the trail always points away from the direction of travel.
