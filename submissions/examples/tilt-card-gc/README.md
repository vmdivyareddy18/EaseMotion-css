# Tilt Info Card UI Example

## 1. What does this do?

This submission creates cards with 3D tilt effects on hover and subtle parallax motion. The cards respond to mouse movement for an interactive, depth-rich experience.

---

## 2. How is it used?

Open `demo.html` directly in your browser. Move your mouse over the cards to see the 3D tilt effect. Copy the `.tilt-card` markup and CSS into your project to add interactive tilting cards.

---

## 3. Why is it useful?

Tilt effects create perceived depth and enhance engagement through interactive motion. The parallax response makes the interface feel more responsive and modern.
