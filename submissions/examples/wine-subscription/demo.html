The Terroiriste Method

Every bottle tells
a story of place

We reject mass-produced, supermarket wines. Our team tastes over 4,000 bottles a year so you only receive the exceptional 1%.

1

Vineyard Sourcing

Our sommeliers travel to 30+ wine regions each year, visiting small-batch estates and building direct relationships with winemakers.

2

Blind Tasting Panels

Every candidate wine is evaluated in a blind panel by our master sommeliers. Only bottles scoring 90+ points advance to member selection.

3

Palate Matching

Your detailed palate profile — built from your onboarding quiz and ongoing ratings — drives our algorithm to select wines you'll love.

4

Delivered, Perfectly

Temperature-controlled packaging ensures every bottle arrives in perfect condition, with tasting notes and a pairing guide in every box.
