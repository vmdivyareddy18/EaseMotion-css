# Ease Animated Team Members Grid

## What does this do?
A responsive grid of team member cards with smooth hover animations revealing social links and bio. On hover the card flips to show the member's bio, role, and social media links. Includes an animated entrance for each card.

## How is it used?
```html
<div class="ease-team-grid">
  <div class="ease-team-card">
    <div class="ease-team-card__inner">
      <div class="ease-team-card__front">
        <div class="ease-team-card__avatar">👩‍💻</div>
        <h3 class="ease-team-card__name">Sarah Johnson</h3>
        <p class="ease-team-card__role">Lead Designer</p>
      </div>
      <div class="ease-team-card__back">
        <p class="ease-team-card__bio">5 years experience in UI/UX design.</p>
        <div class="ease-team-card__socials">
          <a href="#" class="ease-team-card__social">𝕏</a>
        </div>
      </div>
    </div>
  </div>
</div>
```

## Why is it useful?
Team sections are essential on agency and company websites. This fits EaseMotion's animation-first philosophy — animated card reveals and smooth hover effects make team sections feel personal and engaging.
