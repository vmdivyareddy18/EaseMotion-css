# ✊ Rock Paper Scissors

A classic Rock Paper Scissors game built with HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Play against the computer with instant results, track your stats, and challenge yourself to beat your best win streak!

## 🎮 How to Play

1. **Choose your move** – Click Rock (✊), Paper (✋), or Scissors (✌️)
2. **Instant results** – See who wins immediately
3. **Track your stats** – Wins, losses, ties, and best win streak
4. **Beat your record** – Try to get the longest win streak!

## ⌨️ Keyboard Shortcuts

| Key | Action |
|-----|--------|
| `1` or `R` | Play Rock |
| `2` or `P` | Play Paper |
| `3` or `S` | Play Scissors |
| `R` | Reset Stats |

## ✨ Features

- 🎯 **Instant Results** – See who wins immediately
- 📊 **Stats Tracking** – Wins, losses, ties, and best win streak
- 🏆 **Best Win Streak** – Track your longest winning streak
- 💾 **Local Storage** – Stats saved in your browser
- 🎨 **Visual Feedback** – Color-coded results (green for win, red for loss, yellow for tie)
- ⌨️ **Keyboard Shortcuts** – Quick input with number keys
- 📱 **Responsive Design** – Works on desktop and mobile
- 🖐️ **Touch Support** – Play on mobile devices
- 🔄 **Reset Stats** – Start over anytime

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- HTML5
- CSS3 (Flexbox, gradients, responsive design)
- Vanilla JavaScript
