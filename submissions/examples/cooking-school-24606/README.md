# Cooking School Template
A warm, inviting, and appetite-driven template for culinary schools, professional cooking courses, and gourmet workshops.

- **Design**: Uses a "Culinary-Warmth" palette with spice-orange energetic accents, sage-green fresh highlights, and cream-based backgrounds to evoke a professional yet cozy kitchen atmosphere.
- **Use Case**: Culinary arts academies, cooking hobbyist courses, and professional chef certification programs.
