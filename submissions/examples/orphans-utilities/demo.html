Orphans Utility Classes

Control the minimum number of lines of a paragraph that must remain together at the bottom of a column or page before a break, via the orphans property. Most visible in multi-column layouts or when printed (Cmd/Ctrl+P print preview).

💡 Note: orphans only takes effect when content is fragmented across columns or pages. Resize this demo's columns won't show much in a normal browser view — print preview or multi-column text best demonstrates the effect. No orphans/widows declarations exist anywhere in core/ or components/ today.
