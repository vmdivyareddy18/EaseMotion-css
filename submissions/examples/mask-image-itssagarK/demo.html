Mask Image Utilities

Use alpha mask gradients to sculpt the visible region of images and elements.

mask-none

Normal fully visible element without masking.

mask-linear-to-bottom

Smooth transition from solid at the top to transparent at the bottom.

mask-linear-to-right

Smooth transition from solid on the left to transparent on the right.

mask-radial

Circular gradient mask that fades outward from the center.

mask-vignette

A vignette layout keeping edges dark/transparent and the center visible.
