CSS Hyphens Text Layout

Control automatic hyphenation of text content. Prevents ragged right edges in narrow columns and justifies text cleanly without overflow or horizontal scroll.

hyphens: none

No Hyphenation Applied

Supercalifragilisticexpialidocious unpronounceable longword without breaks causing overflow issues in constrained column widths and multi-column newspaper-style layouts.

hyphens: auto

Automatic Hyphenation Enabled

Supercalifragilisticexpialidocious unpronounceable longword breaks gracefully at syllable boundaries to fit column width without causing horizontal overflow or ragged edges.

hyphen-limit-chars: 6 3 2

Strict Hyphenation Constraints

Constrains hyphenation to words with minimum 6 characters, allowing minimum 3 before hyphen, minimum 2 after. Produces clean justified text in narrow newspaper columns.

max-width: 280px narrow

Narrow Sidebar Column

Demonstrating hyphens in a constrained sidebar width where automatic line breaking is essential for readability and preventing text overflow outside the sidebar boundary.
