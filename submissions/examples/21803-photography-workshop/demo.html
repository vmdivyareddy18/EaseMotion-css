Schedule

A paced curriculum with live capture, critique, and refinement.

Each day moves from creative direction to execution, then into curation and finishing so the learning stays tactile and practical.

Day 1

Concept & camera language

  • Visual references, shot lists, and editorial pacing
  • Lens choice, metering, and soft-contrast lighting
  • Live shoot demonstration with tethered review
Day 2

Direction & styling

  • Posing for natural shape and subtle emotion
  • Working with wardrobe texture, skin tone, and movement
  • Small-group shooting with mentor critique
Day 3

Editing & portfolio finish

  • Selection workflow and sequence building
  • Color grading, retouching restraint, and export delivery
  • Portfolio review and premium client positioning

FAQ

Common questions before you apply.

Is this workshop beginner-friendly?

It suits advanced beginners through working professionals. You should be comfortable with manual exposure and basic editing.

Do I need a specific camera brand?

No. Any DSLR or mirrorless system is welcome as long as you can shoot manually and bring one portrait-friendly lens.

Will there be hands-on shooting time?

Yes. The curriculum includes live demonstrations and small-group practice sessions with feedback during the workshop.

Can I attend remotely?

This edition is designed as an in-person studio experience to preserve the critique quality and tactile lighting practice.
