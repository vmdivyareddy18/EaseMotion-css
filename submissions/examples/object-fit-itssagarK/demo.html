Object Fit Utilities

Specify how images and videos should stretch or preserve aspect ratio inside boxes.

object-cover

Fills the entire box, maintaining aspect ratio but cropping edges.

object-contain

Fits fully inside the box, maintaining aspect ratio without cropping.

object-fill

Fills the container fully, stretching and distorting the aspect ratio if needed.

object-none

Ignores container dimensions entirely and displays the image at default size.

object-scale-down

Compares contain and none to pick the version with smaller final size.
