How We Work

Valuation Methodology

Our four-stage process merges rigorous auditing standards with predictive financial analytics.

1

Phase 1: Discovery & Financial Audit

We collect 3-5 years of historical financial statements, corporate tax returns, and current year-to-date ledgers. We perform a complete review of organizational structures and shareholder debt schedules.

2

Phase 2: SDE / EBITDA Normalization

Financials are adjusted to normal market conditions. We identify non-operating cash expenses, excessive shareholder salaries, non-recurring expenses, and owner perks to isolate true economic earnings.

3

Phase 3: Multi-Model Evaluation

We combine the Income Approach (Discounted Cash Flow, capitalization of earnings), the Market Approach (transaction multiples from comparable database datasets), and the Asset Approach to determine fair market value.

4

Phase 4: Certified Report Delivery

A formal, comprehensive valuation report is authored. Fully certified, ABV/ASA appraisal compliance is guaranteed. Reports are formatted specifically for IRS review, litigation filings, or buyer diligence packages.
