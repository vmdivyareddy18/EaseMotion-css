# Anglerfish

A pure CSS and HTML deep sea anglerfish hanging in the black, its bioluminescent lure glowing on a dancing rod above a mouthful of glassy teeth.

## What it shows

- Interlocking teeth from a clip-path sawtooth on both the upper and lower jaw, the lower row chomping with the jaw
- A lure that pulses its own glow with an animated box-shadow, on a rod that sways over the head
- Fins and forked tail cut from clip-path polygons
- A dark round scene with drifting marine snow and rising bubbles for the abyssal setting
- No images, no JavaScript, no external dependencies

## Usage

Open `demo.html` in any modern browser.

## Accessibility

All motion stops under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.
