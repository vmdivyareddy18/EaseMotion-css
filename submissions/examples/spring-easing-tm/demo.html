Spring Easing Functions

Physics-based timing curves for natural-feeling animations

Underdamped cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1) Overshoots then settles. Bouncy.
Critically Damped cubic-bezier(0.22, 1, 0.36, 1) Fast approach, no overshoot. Smoothest.
Overdamped cubic-bezier(0.68, -0.2, 0.27, 1.1) Slow settle, no overshoot. Heavy.
Soft Landing cubic-bezier(0.13, 0.99, 0.54, 0.93) Gentle ease-in, soft ease-out.
