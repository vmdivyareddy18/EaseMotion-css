# Premium Hair Salon & Barbershop Landing Page

A complete, high-fidelity, and fully responsive landing page for a luxury Beauty & Grooming business ("Luxe & Cut") built under issue #14817 using the **EaseMotion CSS** design system.

## EaseMotion Features Showcased

- **Entrance Animations:** Leverages `.ease-reveal` and direction helpers (`.ease-reveal-up`, `.ease-reveal-right`) with scroll-reveal triggers.
- **Hover Micro-interactions:** Showcases `.ease-card-lift` and `.ease-hover-shimmer` on active buttons and interactive menus.
- **Component Systems:** Utilizes standardized `.ease-btn`, `.ease-btn-primary`, `.ease-btn-outline`, and layout classes (`.ease-flex`, `.ease-grid`, `.ease-card`).

## Required Sections Included

1. **Stylish Hero:** Luxe black & gold aesthetic with an instant booking CTA.
2. **Services & Pricing:** A clean menu detailing premium cuts, shaves, and conditioning treatments.
3. **Stylist Profiles:** Highly interactive cards highlighting the master barbers and color specialists.
4. **Before/After Slider:** Visual transition representation showing premium styling transformations.
5. **Instagram Grid:** A stylish photography gallery showcasing client transformations.
6. **Online Booking:** A clean form built with EaseMotion's interactive styles.
7. **Location & Contact Map:** A dark-mode mockup of the salon's physical location.
