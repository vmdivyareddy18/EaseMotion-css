Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our enterprise platform.

How does the usage-based pricing work?

You only pay for the compute resources and bandwidth you actually use. We calculate your usage at the end of each billing cycle down to the millisecond. There are no upfront commitments or hidden minimum fees.

Can I integrate with my existing tech stack?

Absolutely. We offer over 200 native integrations including Slack, Salesforce, GitHub, and Jira. For custom internal tools, our robust GraphQL API allows you to programmatically manage your entire workspace environment.

What level of support is included?

All plans include access to our community forums and extensive documentation. Enterprise plans receive dedicated 24/7 phone support, a guaranteed 1-hour SLA for critical issues, and a dedicated technical account manager.
