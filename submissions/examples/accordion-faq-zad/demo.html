Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our platform

01How does the free trial work?

You get full access to all features for 14 days with no credit card required. At the end of the trial, you can choose a plan that fits your needs. Your data is preserved during the transition.

02Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade at any time. Upgrades take effect immediately, while downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle. Prorated credits are issued for unused time.

03Is my data secure?

We use industry-standard encryption at rest and in transit. All data is stored on SOC 2 compliant servers with regular security audits. We also support SSO and 2FA for additional account protection.

04What kind of support do you offer?

All plans include email support with a response time under 4 hours. Pro and Enterprise plans get priority chat support with 1-hour SLA. Enterprise customers also receive a dedicated account manager.

05Can I integrate with other tools?

We offer native integrations with 200+ popular tools including Slack, GitHub, Jira, and Salesforce. Our REST API and webhooks allow custom integrations with any platform you use.

06Do you have a money-back guarantee?

Absolutely. If you are not satisfied within the first 30 days of your paid subscription, we will issue a full refund. No questions asked. We believe in the quality of our product.
