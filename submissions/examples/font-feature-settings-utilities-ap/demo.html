Accessibility Guidance (WCAG 2.1)

      

        

          
Success Criterion 1.4.4

          
Font Scalability & Readability

          

            When utilizing stylistic sets or alternates (like
            `.font-feature-salt` or `.font-feature-ss01`), make sure they do not
            distort standard letterforms extensively. Users with low vision or
            cognitive differences rely on standard, clear shapes for reading
            comprehension.
          

        


        

          
Typographic Contrast

          
Lining Numbers & Layouts

          

            Oldstyle figures (`.font-feature-onum`) are beautiful for editorial
            bodies, but lining numbers (`.font-feature-lnum`) or tabular figures
            (`.font-feature-tnum`) are much easier to scan in financial
            dashboards and matrices. Structure numbers appropriately.
