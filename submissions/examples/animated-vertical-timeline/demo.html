Our Journey

A look back at the milestones that shaped our company.

2022 - 2023

The Beginning

Launched the initial version of the platform, focusing on core features, user onboarding, and building our first community of early adopters.

2023 - 2024

Global Expansion

Expanded services to 15 new countries, introducing multi-language support, localized payment gateways, and 24/7 global customer support.

2024 - 2025

Series B Funding

Successfully raised $15M in Series B funding, allowing us to double our engineering team and accelerate our product roadmap.

2025 - Present

AI Integration

Integrated advanced AI models to provide predictive analytics, automated workflow optimizations, and personalized user experiences.
