# Firework Display – Click to launch fireworks

> A production-ready firework display component that launches colorful particle fireworks with realistic physics, trails, and full customization. Built with vanilla HTML, CSS, and JavaScript—no dependencies required.

## ✨ Features

- ✅ **Click to launch** – Click anywhere to launch fireworks
- ✅ **Realistic physics** – Gravity, fade, and particle motion
- ✅ **Glow effects** – Soft glow around each particle
- ✅ **Trails** – Particles leave beautiful trails
- ✅ **Customizable** – Count, gravity, fade, color
- ✅ **Auto mode** – Automatic fireworks display
- ✅ **Clear** – Clear all particles instantly
- ✅ **Random colors** – Randomize firework colors
- ✅ **Keyboard shortcuts** – Space (launch), A (auto), C (clear), R (random), T (trails)
- ✅ **Responsive** – Adapts to any screen size
- ✅ **Light/Dark mode** – Auto-adjusts to system preference
- ✅ **Zero dependencies** – No libraries or frameworks
