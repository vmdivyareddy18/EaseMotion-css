# CSS EdTech Course Card Placeholder

This submission details the `.ease-card-course` component, optimized for EdTech courses, tutoring previews, and webinar landing listing pages.

---

## Acceptance Criteria Handled

- **Hover Thumbnail Zoom Overlay:** The card image scales up smoothly by `8%` on hover within its container bounds, while revealing a centered "Preview Course" glass overlay.
- **Category Badge:** Displayed at the top-left of the image to categorize lessons.
- **Course Details:** Includes Course Title, Instructor name, star reviews, and review counts.
- **Call-to-Action:** Layout structure holds original and discounted prices on the left, and a call-to-action enrollment button on the right.

---

## Verification Steps

1. Open `demo.html` in a web browser.
2. Hover over the course listing card. Verify:
   - The card lifts vertically by `4px` with a subtle purple perimeter glow.
   - The image inside scales up slightly.
   - A centered "Preview Course" frosted overlay transitions into view.
3. Check the alignment of the price block and "Enroll Now" buttons.
