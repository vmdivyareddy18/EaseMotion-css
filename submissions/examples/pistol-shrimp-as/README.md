# Pistol Shrimp

A pure CSS and HTML pistol shrimp firing its snapping claw: the dactyl slams shut, a cavitation bubble forms and collapses in a flash of light, and the shockwave stuns a passing fish.

## What it shows

- The snap choreographed on one timeline: the claw cocks, the dactyl slams, a cavitation bubble balloons and implodes, a light flash pops and shockwave rings race out
- The bubble collapsing rather than popping outward, scaling up then crushing to nothing in a few frames
- A segmented carapace and fan tail from repeating linear gradients and a clip-path
- Legs, antennae and the small claw each parking their own angle in a custom property for shared keyframes
- No images, no JavaScript, no external dependencies

## Usage

Open `demo.html` in any modern browser.

## Accessibility

All motion stops under `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`.
