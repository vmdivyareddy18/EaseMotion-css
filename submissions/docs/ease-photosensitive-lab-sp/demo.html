Accessibility guidance

Reduced motion ≠ photosensitive safe

prefers-reduced-motion minimizes motion for vestibular sensitivity. Photosensitive safety also requires avoiding rapid flashing, high-contrast flicker, and large strobing areas — even at slow speeds.

WCAG guidance

  • Avoid more than 3 flashes per second.
  • Keep flashing area small (under 25% of viewport).
  • Never flash pure black ↔ pure white on large surfaces.
  • Limit looping animations with --ease-animation-iterations.

Production checklist

  • Prefer one-shot entrances (ease-fade-in).
  • Cap loops at 2–3 iterations for attention cues.
  • Use static icons instead of infinite spinners when possible.
  • Test with reduced-motion toggle enabled before shipping.
